'So so so happy!' Billie Faiers announces engagement and flashes HUGE rock

July 28, 2024
blog

Not only will she be giving birth to her first child, but her long-term boyfriend has just put a ring on it.

The TOWIE star has announced that she is engaged to boyfriend of two years Greg Shepherd, after the property developer popped the question while on holidays in the Maldives.

Speaking to OK!, Billie gushed: "He said ‘ I love you, I want to spend the rest of my life with you and I know our baby will be just as beautiful as you are. Will you marry me?’

"Then he started crying. It took a while for it all to sink it and then ten minutes later I got a bit tearful too."

It's no wonder the pregnant reality star said yes when you see the size of the sparkler Greg offered in exchange for her hand.

Billie shared the first snap of her ring on Instagram, writing: "So so so happy to be engaged xxx", and let's just say her baby bump won't be the only heavy cargo she'll be toting around.

Greg reportedly got the stunning diamond band made especially for Billie, and proposed in a picturesque sand bank while on holiday.

Billie also shared pictures of a rose-strewn bed, complete with swans, and a romantic petal-filled bath from the night of her engagement.

But the 24-year-old insisted she was oblivious to Greg's plans, and didn't think he was about to pop the question – because the swimming shorts he was wearing had no pockets to hide a ring.

While an engagement is enough to get excited about, Billie has even more joyous news.

The blonde has revealed that she is having a baby girl, with sister Sam excitedly tweeting: "Can't wait to meet my little baby niece love her already".

Billie, who is 20 weeks pregnant, claimed that she wants to have two girls close in age to be just like her and Sam.

Billie's Essex pals and co-stars rushed to Twitter to congratulate the couple, with Lucy Mecklenburgh writing: "A little girl & engaged! So happy for u both" and Ferne McCann tweeting: "Congratulations to my darlings".

Meanwhile, Greg was more miffed that his fiancée revealed in a national magazine that he cried.

A TOWIE baby AND a wedding? The new series is spoiling us already.
