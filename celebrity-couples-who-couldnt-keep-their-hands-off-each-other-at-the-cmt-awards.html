Celebrity couples who couldn't keep their hands off each other at the CMT Awards

blog

The 2024 CMT Music Awards saw all the hottest country couples dressed up for a night of glitz and glamor, and they did not disappoint. In fact, they looked so good, they couldn’t keep their hands off each other.

Although we didn’t get a red carpet debut from Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift, the celebrity couples in attendance put on a show of affection, and we can’t get enough!

Chase Stokes and Kelsea Ballerini

Kelsea Ballerini was the ‘hostess with the mostest’ as she matched the red carpet in her stunning gown, accompanied by boyfriend Chase Stokes.

The two weren’t shying away from the PDA as they shared a passionate kiss in front of the cameras, just over a year after confirming their relationship.

Jelly Roll and Bunnie XO

Love must have been in the air during this award show, as another kiss was shared between Jelly Roll and Bunnie XO.

The couple has been married since 2016, after the singer proposed on stage in Vegas, and it doesn’t look like the passion is dying down anytime soon.

Jett Puckett and Campbell Puckett

The King and Queen of TikTok weren’t going to miss the greatest video opportunity of them all. Before the show, the two posted a flawless transition getting ready into their ball gowns, sharing a kiss at the end.

“Pookie looks absolutely fire tonight,” Jett gushed, as she stunned in a mesh black gown. If that isn’t couple goals, we don’t know what is!

Lainey Wilson and Duck Hodges

The matching cowboy hats got us with these two. Devil Hodges and Lainey Wilson were over the moon to be on the carpet together, we feel like we’re third wheeling even looking at these pics.

The music star stunned in her signature bell-bottoms as her former NFL star boyfriend opted for a safe, but smart black suit.

George Birge and Kara Lee Birge

George was another star who opted for the cowboy hat, but it looks like his wife didn’t want it to cramp her style, although they are in fashion now, just ask Beyonce!

Nonetheless, Kara looked stunning as she sparkled in silver, and the couple, who have been married for more than ten years, were cheered on by cheerleaders as they cutely walked arm in arm down the carpet.
