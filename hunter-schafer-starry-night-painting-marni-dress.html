Hunter Schafer Looks Like a Walking &quot;Starry Night&quot; in This Marni Dress

June 26, 2024
blog

Even if post-impressionism isn’t your thing, it’s hard to deny the loveliness of Hunter Schafer’s latest look.

The Euphoria star attended the 2024 GQ Creativity Awards on Thursday night, where she wore an artsy Marni gown that looked like a reimagining of Vincent van Gogh’s Starry Night. All across the design were thick brushstrokes in alternating shades of indigo and azure, just like a night sky, while random spots of eggshell and buttery yellow resembled glowing orbs, evoking the stars that dot the heavens in van Gogh’s famous painting.

Nina Westervelt//Getty Images

Schafer turned to her frequent stylist, Dara, for the custom look. The stylist posted videos from the event on her Instagram account; in the clips, we can see the dress is super textured, and it looks like the design may have actually been painted right onto the fabric. Other details include a plunging neckline and a body-hugging silhouette that halts right around the hips and transforms into a column skirt. To mirror the front of the dress, the back also has a deep V shape.

Dimitrios Kambouris//Getty Images

The art continued with Schafer’s pointy flats, which featured the same design as the dress. For accessories, the New Jersey native went sans jewelry, except for a pair of dainty diamond drop earrings that featured what appeared to be square citrine gemstones.

At the event, Schafer was honored alongside Formula One racer Lewis Hamilton, actor Danny McBride, and musicians Trent Reznor and Atticus Ross for being some of “the world’s most fearless and inspiring creatives.” If we know one thing, it’s that Schafer is always fearless when it comes to fashion.
