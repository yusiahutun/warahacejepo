Nick Jonas fans 'hope he's okay' after unfortunate onstage tumble

August 28, 2024
blog

Just weeks after brother Joe shared his most embarrassing moment on stage, younger brother Nick had a different kind of accident, as he took a fall whilst on stage in Boston.

The music star is currently on tour with his brothers, as his wife Priyanka Chopra was recently seen overcome with emotion at one of their gigs. This time might have been a whole different story.

Nick Jonas takes a fall onstage

Luckily for us (but maybe not for Nick), we’re in the era where everything is filmed and posted online, so he wasn’t going to let this one slide like he did.

In the video, which is now going viral on Instagram, the 30-year-old can be seen falling through the stage while performing the band’s hit track, Sail Away.

Nick, who sported a white shirt and bright yellow trousers, couldn’t be missed as he took the tumble, from which he quickly recovered, and styled it out in true boyband style.

The star can be seen sitting on the stage for a moment before the fall, before pulling a facial expression that looks like he sees the funny side, before running off to join his brothers again.

This isn’t the first time a celebrity has fallen through the stage. OG Drake fans may remember the time Travis Scott fell through the stage after he made a surprise appearance at The Boy Meets World Tour in London.

Fans hope the star is okay

From the video, it looks like Nick is doing absolutely fine, although concerned fans took to the comments as they hope he wasn’t hurt from the tumble.

“Omg i hope he’s okay,” penned one.

“Poor guy, glad he’s okay and wasn’t worse,” commented another.

One Jonas Brothers fan said: “Aww that could’ve been so much worse than what it was. I’m glad he’s okay.”

However, others also saw the funny side of the fall as one joked: “The fact that he fell during sail away makes it a little funnier than it should be.”
