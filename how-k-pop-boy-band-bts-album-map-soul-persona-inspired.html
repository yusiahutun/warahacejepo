How K-pop boy band BTS’ album Map of the Soul: Persona inspired a gorgeous collection of Korean tableware

KwangJuYo, a traditional Korean ceramics brand, has given a contemporary twist to its tableware by releasing a special edition in tandem with global sensation BTS.

The collection of ceramics, comprising everything from plates and bowls to mugs, is decorated with a delicate pattern of red apricot blossoms, and is inspired by BTS’ album “Map of the Soul: Persona”.

Why Korean fans are upset with BTS and other K-pop groups

This alliance aims to expand the global presence of Korean ceramic products and spread BTS’ positive narrative of hope and love through Korean porcelain products.

The KwangJuYo BTS edition can either be bought at KwangJuYo’s Gahoe-dong shop in Seoul or ordered online through its official online mall.
