Shoppers Call This $21 Foot Balm a Miracle Worker for Dry Heels

April 06, 2024
blog

It’s no secret that the closer we get to winter, the drier our skin gets. As you’re amping up your skincare routine by adding hydrating body moisturizers, serums, and nourishing masks to your lineup, don’t neglect one very important body part: your feet. 

As someone who’s constantly rotating through sneakers, boots, and heels, especially with the onslaught of holiday gatherings, my feet are put through the wringer. While I love to treat myself to the occasional pedicure, it doesn’t always fit into my busy schedule or budget, but my cracked heels still need some TLC. 

I shop Target for a living and recently stumbled upon a product I immediately added to my virtual cart to help revive my winter-stricken feet. The Olive & June Callus Remover Heel Balm is just $21 (far less expensive than what I spend on pedicures), and thanks to the overnight treatment’s glowing five-star reviews, I was convinced to purchase it. 

Olive & June Callus Removal Heel Balm

The vegan formula combines ingredients like sunflower and meadowfoam to soothe and rejuvenate cracked skin, and it’s completely mess-free thanks to its roll-on applicator. Shoppers have called the overnight heel treatment a “miracle worker” within the hundreds of five-star reviews, and one person in particular claims it’ll “magically transform winter feet into smooth, summer-ready feet.” They’ve been using the balm daily for a month and have already seen a difference. Another reviewer attributed this balm to being the only reason their “heels survive winter.” 

While the brand suggests utilizing the balm each night before bed, numerous shoppers have gone the extra mile to lock in moisture while they sleep by wearing fuzzy socks after applying it. And the balm has another hidden benefit, as highlighted by one shopper who shared that it’s a “secret weapon for cuticles” and can be used on your fingers and toes for supple skin.

Grab Olive & June’s Callus Remover at Target like me, and keep scrolling for other shopper-loved nailcare finds from the brand.
