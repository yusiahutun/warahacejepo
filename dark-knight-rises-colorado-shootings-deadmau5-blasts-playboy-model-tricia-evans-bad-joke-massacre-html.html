A Playboy model has caused a backlash after tweeting an offensive joke about the Colorado shootings.

Tricia Evans wrote: 'I hear the new Batman movie is really to die for. Too soon?'

Amongst the first to reply to the blonde was well known Canadian producer Deadmau5, formally known as Joel Zimmerman.

Controversy: Playboy model Tricia Evans received death threats after tweeting a joke about the Colorado shootings

Provocative: Tricia describes herself as a 'controversial tweeter'

Deadmau5 wrote: 'Good to know. @HollywoodTricia I seriously had no idea how big of a piece of s*** you are. Unfollowed.'

His reply brought the tweet to the attention of many other Twitter users and prompted a storm of angry responses, including death threats.

Angry response: Canadian music producer Deadmau5
