See the Most Jaw-Dropping Jewelry

August 15, 2024
blog

"Shine bright like a diamond" would be an understatement in describing the mega-watt shine celebrities brought to the 81st Golden Globes with their massive jewelry. From dazzling diamonds and polished pearls there was no shortage of beautiful bijoux to leaving us permanently starry-eyed.

On the red carpet, Arianna Greenblatt blew onlookers away in a deconstructed tuxedo minus the jacket but including the cumberbund with, accessorizing her Golden Globes look with stunning gems from Lorraine Schwartz. The jewelry suite featured three diamond-accented chains and stacks on stacks of glittering diamond rings. Frankly, we were a bit too dazzled to keep count.

Others opted for more subtle accessorizing that was, nonetheless, remarkable thanks to impeccable styling, including Colman Domingo, who wore Bayard Rustin, Misho, and David Yurman pearl jewelry to lend a vintage vibe to his custom Louis Vuitton suit. Keep scrolling to see the most dazzling jewelry from the 2024 Golden Globes red carpet.

Taylor Swift in De Beers

De Beers decked out the Era's Tour singer in a suite of jewelry that included countless carats and polished platinum and white gold. Her larger pieces were inspired by musical notes from Beethoven’s, Moonlight Sonata

Jennifer Lopez in Boucheron

For the 2024 Golden Globes, Lopez chose to wear creations from Maison Boucheron's "Hopi the hummingbird" collection that featured morganite paved with sapphires and diamonds.

Dua Lipa in Vintage Tiffany & Co.

Dua Lipa didn't just accessorize her Schiaparelli gown with vintage Tiffany & Co. jewelry. She accessorized with archival Tiffany & Co. jewels from 1962.

Lily Gladstone in Antelope Women Designs and Bulgari

Lily Gladstone accessorized her custom Valentino look with a Bulgari necklace with over 53 carats of diamonds and a pair of dazzling earrings. On the red carpet, Gladstone named the designer of her earrings: Blackfeet designer Lenise Omeaso of Antelope Women Designs.

Fantasia Barrino in Ring Concierge and Simon G.

The Color Purple songstress shimmered in jewelry from Ring Concierge. Black velvet gloves lent the perfect contrast for her radiant rings from Simon G.

Ali Wong in Swarovski

The actress made history tonight for her award-winning work in Netflix's Beef. Wong paired her signature glasses with bold, blingy baubles from Swarovski jewels.

Da'Vine Joy Randolph in Omega and Effy

Da'Vine was declared a winner for her supporting work in The Holdovers wearing a classic Omega timepiece and a ruby ring from jewelry brand Effy.

Ariana Greenblatt in Chopard

The subtlety of Greenblatt's deconstructed tuxedo was the perfect backdrop for her major Chopard moment featuring layered diamond necklaces and stacked diamond rings.

Elle Fanning in Cartier

Elle Fanning does timeless elegance better than most actresses and she went for a deliciously classic look with the Cartier necklace and earrings that accessorized her vintage Pierre Balmain gown.

Colma Domingo in Rustin, Misho, and David Yurman

Pearls aren't just for single or stacked strands. Colma Domingo went the eclectic route for the 2024 Golden Globes combining delicate pearl pieces from three jewelry designers — Rustin, Misho, and David Yurman — to bring out the luster in his custom Louis Vuitton suit.

Jennifer Lawrence in Tiffany & Co.

Lawrence paired her pared-back black gown with a showy pear-cut pendant necklace from Tiffany & co, along with feather-like earrings that hugged her lobes.

Elizabeth Banks in Messika

Some stars love pearls, some love diamonds, and some, like Elizabeth Banks rocking Messika jewelry, prefer both if her dazzling earrings and rings are any indication.

Angela Bassett in Lagos

Bassett donned dangling crimson earrings from Lagos and a matching lip color to pair with her Dolce & Gabbana dress.

Helen Mirren in Harry Winston

There are statement earrings and then there are statement earrings like the dazzling Harry Winston chandelier pair Helen Mirren wore to accessorize her equally glamourous Pamella Roland gown.

Margot Robbie in Lorraine Schwartz

Earrings don't have to dangle down to your collarbones to be statement pieces — studs can work some magic too. Case in point: Margot Robbie's glittering Lorraine Schwartz diamond earrings which were breathtaking.

Natasha Lyonne in Schiaparelli

The only thing more deliciously dramatic than Natasha Lyonne's Schiaparelli gown was Natasha Lyonne's pair of Schiaparelli earrings.

Natalie Portman in De Beers

The May December" star wore blinding platinum earrings from De Beers to go along with her beaded Dior Couture dress.

Reese Witherspoon in Tiffany & Co. jewelry

Witherspoon's square-cut diamond earrings and art-deco inspired collar look effortless chic with her Monique Lhuillier ensemble.

Keri Russell in Jacob & Co.

Russell's statement choker blew us away so much that we almost didn't notice her subtle, but stunning, matching ear cuffs.

Selena Gomez in Bulgari

Nominated as Best Actress in Television Series – Musical or Comedy for her performance in Only Murders in the Building, Gomez glistened in showstopping jewels from Bulgari.
