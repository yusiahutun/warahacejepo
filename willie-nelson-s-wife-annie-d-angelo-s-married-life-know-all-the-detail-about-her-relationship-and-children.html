Makeup Artist Annie D'Angelo And Husband Willie Nelson: Happy Couple Together Since 1986

It all started after country music singer Willie Nelson first met his now-wife, Annie D'Angelo on the sets 1986 film Stagecoach. At the time, the latter was makeup artist whereas the former starred in the movie alongside actors Kris Kristofferson and Johnny Cash. During the first encounter, Nelson was still married to his third wife, Connie Koepke.

Following Nelson divorce with third wife Connie Koepke in 1988, he married Annie D' Angelo exchanged on 16 September 1991 in a grand wedding ceremony at Nashville, Tennessee and since then they are living happily ever after.
