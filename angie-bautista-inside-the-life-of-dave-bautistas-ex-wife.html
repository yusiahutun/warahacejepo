What Does Angie Bautista Do?

Like most details about her life, Angie Bautista’s career choice is a mystery. Although reports suggest she worked in fitness, there is no evidence to back the claims up. On the other hand, her ex-husband initially became famous for his career with WWE, where he wrestled under the ring name Batista. 

With WWE, Dave Bautista became a four-time WWE World Heavyweight Champion and two-time WWE Champion. In addition, he is a three-time World Tag Team Champion and a one-time WWE Tag Team Champion. Angie’s ex-husband is also the 2005 and 2014 Royal Rumble winner, and he headlined WrestleMania 21 and WrestleMania XXX. 

However, the former WWE champion debuted as an actor in an uncredited role in the 2006 comedy “Relative Strangers.” Afterward, Dave has since appeared as Drax the Destroyer in the Marvel Cinematic Universe film, “Guardians of the Galaxy.” He also appeared as Mr. Hinx in the James Bond film “Spectre,” and as Sapper Morton in “Blade Runner 2049.”

When Did Dave Bautista and Angie Bautista Meet?

Due to the former couple’s cagey lifestyle, there are no details regarding when they started dating. However, Angie Bautista and Dave tied the knot on November 16, 1998. The renowned actor’s collection reportedly started when he bought his ex-wife, Angie, an E.T. lunchbox, and she did not want to break it, so he bought a second one. However, his favorite is the 1967 Green Hornet lunchbox with a picture of Bruce Lee on it. 

Before his marriage to Angie, Dave had been married once. His first wife was Glenda Fe Bautista, whom he married on March 25, 1990. They have two daughters, Keilani (1990) and Athena (1992). However, Dave and Glenda finalized their divorce on April 1, 1998. 

How Many Children Does Dave Bautista’s Ex-Wife Have?

Angie Bautista has a child with the former WWE star. During their marriage, the former couple welcomed a son, Oliver Bautista, in 2007. Angie’s son, Oliver Bautista, was born through in-vitro fertilization (IVF) while she was in remission. Hence, she calls Oliver her “Miracle Baby.” Before the birth of her son, Angie was diagnosed with ovarian cancer on September 9, 2002. 

Angie went in and out of remission in the ensuing years multiple times. About her experience battling cancer, Angie stated that cancer doesn’t look like one thing. She elaborated, “People think that people with cancer look a certain way, but cancer can affect someone who appears perfectly physically fit.”

After her treatments started, Dave traveled across the United States to make a video to raise money for ovarian cancer research. Released in August 2010, the renowned actor dedicated the video, Bautista vs Cancer, to Angie.
