You can now propose with 25k 'Haribo' ring that's encrusted with 224 diamonds

August 26, 2024
blog

Many of us joke about proposing with Haribo rings.

But if we received one of the sweets instead of an actual sparkler, we’d probably be annoyed.

So to jazz up the gummy rings, bespoke jewellery brand Taylor & Hart has created a luxurious design.

It’s the same colour scheme as the Haribos but is topped with an 18 carat yellow gold basket of 40 diamonds and a 2.70 carat cabochon yellow sapphire.

As it contains a whopping 224 diamonds in total, it’s no surprise the engagement ring retails at a hefty £25,000.

Kate Earlam-Charnley, Design Director at Taylor & Hart said: “With Valentine’s Day just around the corner, we thought it was the perfect time to bring this brand new design to life."

So why did Taylor & Hart choose to make this Haribo-inspired ring as its latest bespoke design?

Since its release in 1995, Haribo Starmix has been a firm favourite of Brits, and in 2019 was named the top sweet treat in the UK.
