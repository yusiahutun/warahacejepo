Easter Bunny Phone Number - How to Call the Easter Bunny

February 07, 2024
blog

Early stories of the Easter bunny stretching back centuries tell of a rabbit who lays nests of colorful eggs for well-behaved children. That's not too far off from today's traditions of surprise-filled Easter baskets and epic egg hunts, but even a magical hare can change with the times. Nowadays, kids not only get to track the Easter bunny but can call him too, and you get to be the cool parent (or grandparent) who has the Easter bunny’s phone number.

Dualverse, Inc.

It's all possible thanks to an app called "Call Easter Bunny," which lets your children chat with the famous rabbit. It's a great way to remind your kids to be good because the Easter Bunny is only a phone call away, and it's a sure way to get them excited for the holiday (as if picking out special Easter outfits and putting together super-cute Easter Bunny crafts weren't enough!). Your little ones will always remember the sweet phone conversation they'll have with ole E.B.

So how exactly does the "Call Easter Bunny" app work? You won't be dialing the Easter bunny's phone number the old-fashioned way. Instead, once you download the app to your phone, you'll see an option to click "call," which will connect your kids directly to the Easter Bunny. Or, if you buy the premium version, you can set a time to receive the "call" and the Easter Bunny will call you. When the call comes in, your kiddos will hear a recording from the app's Easter Bunny, a professional impressionist (it sounds authentic!).

Dualverse, Inc.

In the recording, the Easter Bunny says hello to your child, asks what his or her name is, and then reminds them to keep behaving well—it's truly adorable!

The best part about the free Easter Bunny call app? Parents can go back into its password-protected "Parents Only" recording archive to replay the phone conversation with the Easter Bunny. You can even save the sweet phone calls to your camera roll!

To download the free app, search for "Call Easter Bunny" in the Apple app store or in the Google Play store if you have an Android device.
