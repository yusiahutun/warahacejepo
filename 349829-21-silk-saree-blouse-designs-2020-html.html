What is a saree blouse?

A saree no matter the colour, design or pattern must have a blouse. In most cases, the saree blouse, also known as a Choli has a crop top design that shows a big or small mid-section. Older women prefer a blouse to leave a small mid-section while millennials or youth often go for a design that is high enough to show off their toned waist.

Whether you prefer to show more or less skin the best and latest saree blouse designs are all about expressing personal style, confidence in your body, femininity and love for Indian tradition.

Which blouse is best for saree?

There is no one size fit all, the best silk saree blouse designs for you depends on your body type. Hence the top has to show your best feature. For instance, those with short-sleeved and spaghetti straps complement stunning arms and short blouses that fit above the belly button re easy to pull off when you have a flat tummy.

How can I look slim in a saree?

When you are not in your best shape, it’s easy to wonder if there is a miracle to looking slim instantly. Tricks to look slim in a saree are similar to when you are wearing any other outfit. For starters, choose dark tones over light ones, go for crepe silk and georgette over satin, cotton or too clingy fabrics. The blouse should not be too tight or too loose, yet small prints give a slimming effect. Finally, drape the saree in an open loose pallu style.
