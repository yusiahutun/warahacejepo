Hailey Bieber Channels Audrey Hepburn in a Black Dress and Pearls

February 20, 2024
blog

Last night, Hailey Bieber seemed to take a style cue from Audrey Hepburn's star-turning role in the 1961 film, Breakfast at Tiffany's.

Fittingly, the model channeled her inner Hepburn as one of the A-list guests at Tiffany & Co.'s grand reopening of their flagship New York City store. For the occasion, she wore a black midi dress with a curved neckline and a structured waist from Versace's fall/winter 2023 ready-to-wear collection (Emily Ratajkowski debuted the sleek piece on the runway during the house's March show).

She paired the dress with glossy black heeled sandals, and further accessorized with layers of pearl strand necklaces and multiple pearl-drop earrings. She swept her hair up into a tight side-parted high bun, which was fastened with a black bow, and completed the look with a Tiffany-blue manicure and pedicure.

Taylor Hill//Getty Images

Taylor Hill//Getty Images

The party look is her latest to be centered around a sophisticated black dress.

Most recently, the Rhode founder went out for a date night with husband Justin Bieber while wearing a little black dress with a structured halter that hung around the model's neck like a thick collar necklace. At the front, the neckline of the dress curved downwards, sloping into a low-back silhouette. She finished the look with black suede pumps, ultra-sheer tights from Calzedonia, a black patent leather shoulder bag from Saint Laurent, and tiny gold hoop earrings.

She wore another LBD earlier this month during a night out in New York City. Then, she looked fabulous in a strapless mini with a sparkling golden buckle at the bust. She once again styled the look with sheer tights and black pumps.
