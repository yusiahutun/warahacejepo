Amal Clooney's Orange Ombr Sequin Dress Is Fire Flames

September 25, 2024
blog

It's no secret that Amal Clooney has a fire fashion sense, but her most recent look made that obvious in a much more literal sense. While attending the 2023 Fashion Awards presented by Pandora in London on Monday, the human rights attorney was captured burning up the red carpet in an orange ombré dress that literally resembled a flame.

The striking column gown had smaller gold sequins along the bodice that turned into larger, oval-shaped paillettes farther down the dress. The sparkly frock also featured a tank-styled neckline and a long train that trailed behind Clooney. She accessorized with a coordinating gold clutch, diamond drop earrings, and a collection of rings.

Her luscious caramel-highlighted was styled in a deep side part and loose curls. Her glam included a dewy complexion, feathered lashes, and a deep mauve lip, courtesy of Charlotte Tilbury, who she presented the Special Recognition Award for Contribution to the Fashion Industry to on stage later that night.

Amal went stag on the red carpet, arriving without her husband, George Clooney, though she was certainly still in good company. She was joined by a star-studded guest list that included Gwyneth Paltrow, Anne Hathaway, Rita Ora, Taylor Russell, Ashley Park, Charli XCX, and more.

Although George did not accompany his wife on Monday night, the two had a date night on Sunday at the London premiere of George's upcoming film The Boys In The Boat. For the outing, Amal looked both sexy and sophisticated in a black velvet corset bustier and matching wide-leg trousers. For his part, George matched Amal in a simple black suit. At one point, it began to rain during the event, so George held a clear umbrella over his wife's head, like a true gentleman.
