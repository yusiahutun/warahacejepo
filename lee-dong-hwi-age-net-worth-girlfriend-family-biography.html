Lee Dong-hwi Career

In 2013, Dong-hwi made his acting debut in a small part of the movie Run to the South. He appeared in supporting roles in Cold Eyes, Queen of the Night, and Way Back Home that same year. He appeared in movies including Tazza: The Hidden Card, Animal, No Tears for the Dead, and Fashion King in 2014.

In the 2014 KBS2 drama Gunman in Joseon, Dong-hwi made his television debut as Han Jung-hoon. He played Lee Kyung in the SBS series Divorce Lawyer in Love the next year. In the tv series Reply 1988, where he played Ryu Dong-ryong, he made his debut. He has since been in TV shows including Red Teacher, Entourage, Radiant Office, Pegasus Market, and many more.

In movies like Veteran, The Handmaiden, One Line, The Bros, Exit, Confidential Assignment, New Trial, The Call, and New Year Blues, Dong-hwi has won several awards. He is most known, though, for his role as Young-ho in the 2019 movie Extreme Job. He made his MSG Wannabe debut in May 2021.
