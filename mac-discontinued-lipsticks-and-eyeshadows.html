MAC is bringing back discontinued lipsticks &amp; eyeshadows from the 90s

February 14, 2024
blog

BEST. NEWS. EVER. The bosses at MAC have gone through the vaults to find the discontinued products fans are still moaning about, and yes, they’re ALL back! We all know the heartache when our favourite beauty product bids farewell, but thankfully the beauty brand is reuniting us with some of the best from the archives. Terry Barber, MAC’s Director of Makeup Artistry said: “We get so many requests to bring back iconic colours from the past as everyone has a story about when they bought their first MAC product. These eye and lip colours, dating as far back as the early 90s were total game changers when they were originally released. What’s interesting is that they haven’t dated in the slightest!”

L-R: Shrimpton, Skew, Marrakesh, Icon, Bronx

So are you excited to see if your old faithful made the list? Seven lipsticks are being brought back and four eye shadows are being let out the vault. You’ll see the word ‘frost’ a couple of times - which will give you a big clue about the beauty trends going full circle. 

RELATED: MAC drops new Glow range & it must have been created by wizards

Say hey to long-lost loves, lip shades Marrakesh (a warm matte red), Spanish Fly (a reddish brown with green pearlised pigments), Rocker (brown toned burgundy), Bronx (muddy taupey brown), Shrimpton (beige), Skew (pinky nude frost) and Icon (a grey frosty taupe).

Eyeshadow lovers, it’s time to be reunited with shadows Uninterrupted (a burnt yellow), Kid (fleshy nude), Tete-A-Tint (peachy soft neutral), and Goldbit (shimmery gold soft beige), either individually or unified in one perfectly fit Pro Palette.

L-R: Uninterupted, GoldBit Lustre, Kid Veluxe, Tet-A-Tint Matte

The collection of shades are available now, but there’s no need to rush because these products are here to STAY. Praise the makeup heavens!
