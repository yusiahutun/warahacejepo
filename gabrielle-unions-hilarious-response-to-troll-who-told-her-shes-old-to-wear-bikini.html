Gabrielle Union's hilarious response to troll who told her she's 'old' to wear bikini

May 26, 2024
blog

Gabrielle Union shared her hilarious response to a social media troll who once told her she was “too old” to wear a thong bikini.

The American movie actress is unapologetic about her stunning appearance and certainly not a celebrity to hold back her thoughts when she is the target of nasty online comments. Gabrielle revealed her response to one netizen who made a comment about women wearing swimsuits at the age of 50.

Gabrielle Union claps back at troll for bikini comment

The Bring It On actress is proud of her fit figure, which she manages with grueling workouts, and doesn’t shy away to call out trolls who have remarks about the choice of her swimwear.

In an interview with editor Jasmine Fox-Suliaman on the podcast Who What Wear, the 50-year-old Hollywood star revealed that she was told she was “too old” to wear thong bikinis.

“Somebody said, ‘You’re old enough to be somebody’s grandma, why are you still in — like wearing swimsuits?’” she recalled in the podcast episode. “I’m like, ‘First of all, they’re not just swimsuits. They are thongs. And you’re gonna see these cheeks until my ass literally falls off, and I might just wear a bikini in the casket.”

The actress, who stars in the new Netflix rom-com The Perfect Find, is the cover star of Who What Wear’s July 2023 edition.

The actress gave a powerful message

Elsewhere in the podcast interview, Gabrielle gave a powerful message to all listeners and highlighted society’s remarks and the way some people treat women when they reach a certain age.

“(When you’re) 35, they start treating you like you’ve got osteoporosis,” she said. “You start becoming invisible, and it’s a weird, empty feeling to feel like you’re disappearing in front of your own eyes. You start taking on the same attitude as a society that believes that you lack value.”

Gabrielle continued: “I don’t want anyone else to feel that invisibility and that sense of worthlessness because the only people that we need to validate us are us. And sometimes, the more that you validate your damn self, the more people are like, ‘Yeah.’”

The actress has blessed her Instagram fans and followers with pictures from her holidays with her husband Dwayne Wade and their children. The celebrity family was recently in Egypt and Gabrielle shared a string of snaps from the sun-kissed vacation.

Gabrielle’s family with Dwayne Wade

The former America’s Got Talent judge is married to former NBA star Dwayne Wade. They were first romantically linked in 2008 and tied the knot in 2014 in Miami, Florida.

Dwayne was already a father of three children when they married and the couple welcomed a daughter via surrogate in November 2018. The 10 Things I Hate About You actress was married to NFL player Chris Howard from 2001 to 2005.
