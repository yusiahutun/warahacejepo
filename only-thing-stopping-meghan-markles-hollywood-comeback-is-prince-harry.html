Only thing stopping Meghan Markle's Hollywood comeback is 'Prince Harry'

September 26, 2024
blog

A close friend of the late Princess Diana has claimed Prince Harry is holding back his wife from furthering her career. He believes Meghan Markle’s dreams of Hollywood are down to the Duke of Sussex.

Richard Kay, who was a friend of the Princess of Wales, says that Prince Harry is holding back Meghan Markle when it comes to her return to acting. Rumors of the Duchess wanting to break back into showbiz have been swirling for some time. However, it could be her husband and his royal family across the pond that makes it a bigger issue than both could have predicted.

Meghan Markle dreams of Hollywood is down to Prince Harry

When the star joined the royal family and married her husband in 2018, she had already wrapped up her time on Suits. Meghan Markle had left behind her career in Hollywood when she said the words ‘I do’.

Richard Kay says the reason she hasn’t entered back into the spotlight is because of her husband. GB News reported on his comments and discussed them with Kinsey Schofield, a royal commentator and showbiz journalist.

Schofield said: “This is a great point made by one of Princess Diana‘s closest friends, towards the end of her life. His name is Richard Kay, and he told Palace Confidential that the only thing standing in Meghan [Markle]’s way of her big Hollywood comeback is Prince Harry.”

She continued that it wasn’t just her acting that was impacted. 

“He says it’s harder for Meghan to launch her new career objectives amidst all of the royal rift headlines,” Kinsey added. “And again, with all of this litigation, it’s hard for Meghan to come out full force trying to promote the TIG. Everybody wants to focus on the relationship with Harry’s family.”

Negative publicity

The negative press coverage that Schofield may be referring to is the fact that Harry and Meghan were rumored to have snubbed the King’s invite to his 75th birthday celebrations. However, that was later denied by the couple, who say they were actually never invited in the first place.

A spokesperson for the Sussexes told the MailOnline:  “There has been no contact regarding an invitation to His Majesty’s upcoming birthday. It is disappointing The Sunday Times has misreported this story.”

A source further said: “They had not received any invitation and were unaware of any celebrations until the stories came out. I’m sure the Duke will find a way to reach out privately to wish His Majesty a happy birthday like he has always done.”

King Charles “deeply hurt” by Harry’s actions

Sources say that Harry’s actions of snubbing the invitation have “lasting effects” on the head royal.

A source said: “When he gets to the end of another successful work day and thinks of his family, there will always be a twinge in his heart about a problem he hasn’t been able to solve yet,” the insider offered, talking about the lasting effects.”

However, they continued: “There is the sense of a cooling-off period from the family that is under way after the aftershocks of the book and interviews. But that doesn’t change the King’s love for his son.

“He’ll never not invite his son to a family gathering, because that’s not who he is.”
