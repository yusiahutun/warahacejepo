If you’ve been following Sofia Richie Grainge since she joined TikTok in 2023, you’ll know that she has since become one of the biggest names connected to the “quiet luxury” aesthetic. If everyone’s eyes weren’t already on her luxuriously chic OOTDs, they are now, because the model recently announced she’s expecting a baby with her husband, Elliot Grainge. Itching to see how she’s been dressing her bump? We did a deep dive into Sofia’s pregnancy style, and several of the pieces are on sale right now.

While her looks still embody the “stealth wealth” aesthetic, in this new sartorial era, they spotlight much cozier and baggier silhouettes. Long, oversized coats, elegant sunglasses, casual sneakers, and structured bags have become the model’s uniform while flaunting her bump. These essentials come from brand names you’d expect from the queen of quiet luxury. We’re talking The Row, Bottega Veneta, Isabel Marant, Max Mara, and more. On top of that, the style icon has been rocking monochrome outfits in addition to pops of color here and there.

Even though Sofia is known to dress bougie with a capital “B,” during her pregnancy, she’s been gravitating towards comfy, affordable sneakers like the everyone-and-their-grandma-owns-them adidas Sambas. And if you’ve been eyeing the R13 green overalls she recently donned but don’t have $500+ to spare, we wrote a whole other story to share look-alikes that you can add to your closet right this second.

Ahead, find seven looks that define Sofia Richie’s pregnancy style, and be sure to act fast on the designer pieces that are currently marked down (peep the Isabel Marant sunglasses that are a whopping 60 percent off).
