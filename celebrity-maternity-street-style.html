The Best Celebrity MaternityLooks

February 19, 2024
blog

With all the news that 2020 has brought us, celebrity pregnancy announcements have been some of the most exciting. Emily Ratajkowski! Kelly Rowland! Emma Roberts! It seems as if everyone in Hollywood is suddenly having a baby.

While typical red carpets have been limited this year due to the pandemic, we have seen these women step out in stellar street style looks, which often help to highlight the happy news. Whether you're simply a fan or are searching for outfit ideas during your own pregnancy, check out how celebrities have been styling their bumps in 2020 and recent years for a dose of inspiration.

Sophie Turner, February 2020

For a Los Angeles outing with her hubby Joe Jonas, Turner styled a Rolling Stones tee under some baggy overalls, nailing the comfy-cute vibe.

Emma Roberts, October 2020

The actress was spotted bumping along in a polka dotted skirt and long duster jacket. The most notable part of her outfit? The mask, obviously.

Chloë Sevigny, February 2020

Back in February the stylish new mama glowed at the Writers Guild Awards in a black dress and completed the look with gold accents in her jewelry and purse.

Jenna Dewan, January 2020

This gorgeous mama took to the streets of LA and gave off major boho vibes in a flowing, off-the-shoulder dress and sunhat.

Jodie Turner-Smith, January 2020

Another spectacular outfit from the queen. Turner-Smith strutted the streets of London in this whole color blocked lewk, complete with bold tights. The embellished oval glasses topped things off perfectly. (Shop a similar pair here.)

Katy Perry, September 2020

The singer and Daisy's mama took the stage at the Gracie Awards in a vibrant purple and white polka dotted dress.

Sophie Turner, March 2020

While out with Jonas, the expectant mom played it cool in a retro Giants sweatshirt and windbreaker pants.

Jodie Turner-Smith, January 2020

Queen & Slim star went classic and sleek in all-black ensemble. Her growing bump hid behind her long black coat.

Brie Bella, February 2020

The Total Bellas star was spotted showing off her bump in a cheetah mini dress, tights, and slouchy boots.

America Ferrera, 2020

The actress chose silk to accentuate her growing bump. She accompanied her forest green slip dress with a black silk blazer for a very sophisticated look.

Ciara, February 2020

Ciara absolutely nailed the barely-there dress look with this sexy, beaded dress that showed off her bump.

Kelly Rowland, October 2020

Kelly Rowland visited in a friend in a body-hugging, striped T-shirt dress, which could easily be dressed up later on with a blazer and bold accessories.

America Ferrera, February 2020

America Ferrera opted for color and print at the NHMC Impact Awards Gala, wearing a floor-length cheeta gown.

Jodie Turner-Smith, February 2020

We're starting with Jodie Turner-Smith, because how could we not? This Gucci dress is basically the maternity dress of all maternity dresses, immediately grabbing out attention due to its sparkling, vibrant color.

Cardi B, April 2018

Cardi B slipped on a silky striped pants set and showed extra skin — and her growing belly! — by leaving her top open.

Jodie Turner-Smith, January 2020

At the UK Premiere of Queen & Slim, Turner-Smith was dripping elegance and looking like the goddess she is in this cream silk gown.

Kate Hudson, April 2018

Meghan Markle, February 2019

Ashley Graham, September 2019

Hilaria Baldwin, April 2018

Khloé Kardashian, March 2018

Candice Swanepoel, March 2018

Miranda Kerr, February 2018
