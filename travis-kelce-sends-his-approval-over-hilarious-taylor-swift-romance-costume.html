Travis Kelce sends his approval over hilarious Taylor Swift romance costume

June 12, 2024
blog

Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift are taking over the internet right now with their budding romance, and it looks like the NFL star has the humor to play along with the jokes and memes as he reacted to a ‘hilarious’ Taylor Swift costume.

The celebrity couple have been spotted together a number of times over the past few weeks, and with Halloween approaching, it looks like everyone wants to be just like them. Here’s one early Halloween costume that had Kelce ‘in tears.’

Travis Kelce ‘approves’ of hilarious Taylor Swift costume

Travis is known for his bold outfits, however, it was the Taylor Swift costume that got all the attention this time.

Taking to Instagram, user kidsuper posted a video of himself dressed as the music star, in a red skirt and white top, while his friend dressed as the NFL star with a beige and blue two-piece.

They recreated the video of the two walking past and greeting the camera, and in the caption, penned: “TMZ REPORTS ALL THE CELEBS ARE READY FOR TONIGHT!!! Best party of the year! Costumes mandatory!! See you all soon I’m ready to shake it off!!”

As well as having friends and fans laughing in the comments, the content creator even got a message from Travis Kelce himself, who wrote: “[four laughing emojis] you’re the [goat emoji].

The pair were once again seen holding hands as they left the Chiefs game in Kansas City on Sunday following their victory.

It looks like the couple are now starting to coordinate outfits, as they were both seen stunning in red, with the Love Story singer representing the Kansas City Chiefs with a sweatshirt.

Halloween costumes of the couple sell out online

It looks like way more fans are having the idea to dress up as the celebrities for Halloween, as couple costumes of the two have reportedly been selling out online.

Halloween retailer, 3 Wishes, told TMZ that the costumes of Trav and Tay are ‘by far’ the most popular costumes of the year, even selling out.

The retailer told the publication that the costumes are their second fastest-selling in history, coming just behind the Tiger King costume, in 2020.

However, the store was going to get some more stock in, so it’s worth trying your luck. If not, a homemade outfit can do just the trick!
