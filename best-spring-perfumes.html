Most Cult-Favorite

Dior Miss Dior Parfum

Most Cult-Favorite

Dior Miss Dior Parfum

Credit: Dior
Pros
  • Great for floral and citrus scent lovers

It's hard to go wrong with wearing Miss Dior in any season, but especially in spring. Bazaar editors love its fruity-floral blend of mandarin and jasmine accords, along with lily of the valley, centifolia rose, and soft musk.

One satisfied customer writes: "I absolutely love this perfume. Long lasting, soft yet beautiful smelling. This is the best-smelling perfume. I will never use another perfume." —lupitaa4

Best for Chance Lovers

Chanel Chance Eau Fraîche Eau de Parfum

Best for Chance Lovers

Chanel Chance Eau Fraîche Eau de Parfum

Credit: Chanel
Pros
  • Great for fresh and woody fragrance lovers

If you count Chanel's Chance eau de parfum among your go-to scents, this slightly woodier version from the collection might become your new spring staple. It features a blend of citron accord and jasmine, along with notes of teakwood and amber that add to its depth.

Best Overall

Maison Margiela Replica Springtime In a Park Eau de Toilette

Best Overall

Maison Margiela Replica Springtime In a Park Eau de Toilette

Credit: Sephora
Pros
  • Great for floral and fruit fragrance lovers
Cons
  • Some reviewers say it can smell a bit soapy

Here's a perfume that hits the spring season right on the nose: Maison Margiela's Replica line has the uncanny ability to expertly tap into scent memory, which includes this seasonal pick. Pear and lily of the valley accords combine with musk to channel the scent of blossoms in Shanghai in 2019.

One satisfied customer writes: "Smells like you’re holding lilies on a rainy day while walking out of a flower shop. It’s an elegant, soft, clean-like floral with the most impressive longevity I’ve experienced in a perfume." —Femme1

Best Luxe

Maison Francis Kurkdjian Feminin Pluriel Eau de Parfum

Best Luxe

Maison Francis Kurkdjian Feminin Pluriel Eau de Parfum

Credit: Bergdorf Goodman
Pros
  • A little goes a long way
Cons
  • Scent may be too sweet for some

If Baccarat Rouge is one of your winter go-tos, consider swapping in Maison Francis Kurkdjian's floral Feminin Pluriel eau de parfum for spring. It features a blend of four flowers—iris, jasmine, rose, and orange blossom—which means you're essentially embodying a modern floral bouquet.

One satisfied customer writes: "This fragrance is beyond a beautiful scent. It truly smells like stargazer lilies in a floral shop; therefore, a small amount goes a long way. I purchased the 2.4-ounce size online and probably will not use the bottle within a year. Once you spray, let it blossom before spraying more to capture the true smell." —Karamelkarma

Best Earthy Floral

Marc Jacobs Daisy Wild Eau de Parfum

Best Earthy Floral

Marc Jacobs Daisy Wild Eau de Parfum

Credit: Ulta
Pros
  • Refillable

Top of Medina's fragrance list for spring is the earthy-floral Daisy Wild by Marc Jacobs. "It has an addictive macadamia vetiver note quite exquisite with a beautiful jasmine and banana blossom. A must-try," she adds.

One satisfied customer writes: "I really loved this scent, to me it was light and airy. The smell was not overpowering and really reminded me of spring. I feel like it lasted most of the day, usually with one reapplication sometime throughout the day." —Ellalouise00

A trio of cardamom, mimosa, and vanilla milk puts Not Your Baby's powdery scent in the warm florals category—with a surprising, spicy twist of sandalwood.

One satisfied customer writes: "Soft and subtle warm sent—if you love bergamot and sandalwood, with a splash of soft warm vanilla—you’ll love this Phlur scent." —BrittBonds

Best Fruit-Forward

Creed Spring Flower Eau de Parfum

Best Fruit-Forward

Creed Spring Flower Eau de Parfum

Credit: Bergdorf Goodman
Pros
  • Great daily scent

Although the name of this scent is Spring Flower, it actually contains plenty of fruit notes, like peach, apricot, and apple, that lend to its robust blend. The Creed scent is also anchored by jasmine and white flowers, along with musk and ambergris to round it out.

Best Rose

Dries Van Noten Raving Rose Eau de Parfum

Best Rose

Dries Van Noten Raving Rose Eau de Parfum

Credit: Saks Fifth Avenue
Pros
  • Refillable

Distinctive rosa water and rose absolute notes are the clear stars of this popular perfume. But to keep its blend a bit more unexpected, the notes are nicely sandwiched between a combination of black and pink peppers and warm musk.

One satisfied customer writes: "A rose smells like no other... Girly yet powerful, fresh yet intense, soft yet spicy. Whenever I wear this perfume people always ask me what's my fragrance. Really in love with this smell." —Louise

Best Classic Floral

Ellis Brooklyn Florist Eau de Parfum

Best Classic Floral

Ellis Brooklyn Florist Eau de Parfum

Credit: Nordstrom
Pros
  • Great for citrus and floral lovers
Cons

Ellis Brooklyn's Florist eau de parfum combines tuberose petals, golden gardenia, and sweet honeysuckle for an olfactory bouquet that's not too overpowering for everyday wear.

One satisfied customer says: "This perfume has a wonderful floral smell. I feel like it's the beginning of spring. It's such a delicate and pleasant scent. It's a perfect scent for me to wear during the day. a scent that makes my day flowery and joyful." —Leticia B.

Best Citrus

Henry Rose Windows Down Eau de Parfum

Best Citrus

Henry Rose Windows Down Eau de Parfum

Credit: Sephora
Pros
  • Clean at Sephora
Cons
  • Scent may be too light for some users

If florals aren't your preferred spring perfume of choice, we recommend adding this citrusy Henry Rose scent to your collection. This beloved pick blends grapefruit, jasmine, and musk notes with more gourmand honeyed neroli and soft Earl Grey tea.

One satisfied customer writes: "I was not prepared to be overwhelmed and get emotionally attached to a fragrance so clean, fresh, and floral that is also not obnoxious in any way. The first Scent that came to mind was Clinique Happy, although Happy has more accentuated citrus notes, Windows Down literally smells like a clean, crisp meadow in the mornings of summer, followed by a welcoming musk that just makes you want to take a large satisfying whiff lol!" –SoaringHigh

Best Fruit Floral

Burberry Her Eau de Parfum

Best Fruit Floral

Burberry Her Eau de Parfum

Credit: Ulta Beauty

This Burberry fragrance uses notes of crushed berries alongside violet and jasmine for a fruity floral finish. And since its distinctive flower petal bottle is limited-edition, it's also a great pick if you're looking to snap up a perfume that few others have.

One satisfied customer writes: "I fell in love with this perfume as soon as I sprayed it in the store. It definitely is like a soft and warm smell, you can really smell the jasmine and dark berries in there which makes a great combination! Definitely recommend to everyone!" —Burr

Best for Flowerbomb Fans

Viktor & Rolf Flowerbomb Tiger Lily Eau de Parfum

Best for Flowerbomb Fans

Viktor & Rolf Flowerbomb Tiger Lily Eau de Parfum

Credit: Sephora
Pros
  • Easy to layer with other scents
  • Great for spring and summer
Cons

Here's a twist on the cult-fave Flowerbomb (which we've named one of the best perfumes of all time). This version combines heart notes of coconut milk and tiger lily, plus, mango at its base, for a more solar take than its original.

One satisfied customer says: "This perfume has an amazing floral smell, not too sweet, the perfect summer fragrance. It reminds me of the beach! It is very long-lasting and I keep getting compliments." —Cami74

Best Fresh Floral

Guerlain Aqua Allegoria Flora Salvaggia Eau de Toilette

Best Fresh Floral

Guerlain Aqua Allegoria Flora Salvaggia Eau de Toilette

Credit: Sephora
Pros
  • Easy to layer
Cons
  • Watermelon smells a bit artificial when dry.

The gorgeous bottle is just the start of Aqua Allegoria Flora Salvaggia's allure. The lavender juice contains notes of fresh watermelon, wildflower accord, iris, and violet for a powdery finish. Sophisticated noses may also pick up on its blend of white flowers.

One satisfied customer writes: "Summer breeze in a glass. So fresh and clean but not overpoweringly floral. This is feminine and elegant. Good for wear to a luncheon or on a picnic (especially with the watermelon tones)!" —tfaz0912

Best Floral Musk

Kate Spade New York Bloom Eau de Toilette

Best Floral Musk

Kate Spade New York Bloom Eau de Toilette

Credit: Ulta Beauty
Pros
  • Great for fresh and floral fragrance lovers

One spritz reveals this toilette's fresh, floral notes that instantly draw you in. Picture walking through a cloud of granny smith apple, orange essence, and white camelia notes. And once the fragrance dries down, its cedarwood and sandalwood base really stands out.

One satisfied customer writes: "The green apple is prominent but also layered with a fresh bouquet-like floral heart that gives the fragrance its signature. This is the definition of a spring fragrance good on the brand releasing it accordingly. The apple has a persistent bitter edge to it that is done very well and also possesses a surprising crispness, quite a nice release overall from Kate Spade!" —RhettB27

By Kilian Flower of Immortality Eau de Parfum

Best Floral Gourmand

By Kilian Flower of Immortality Eau de Parfum

Credit: Bergdorf Goodman
Pros

Flower of Immortality eau de parfum showcases the softer side of the often powerful By Kilian fragrance blends. Key notes of white peach, iris butter, and tonka bean keep it grounded, while freesia, rose, and vanilla lend to its sweeter finish.
