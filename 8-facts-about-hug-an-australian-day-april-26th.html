What is Hug an Australian Day?

Hug an Australian Day, celebrated on April 26th, is a unique occasion dedicated to showing affection towards Australians. This day encourages people worldwide to give Australians a hug, symbolizing friendship and appreciation for their culture and contributions. It's not just about physical hugs; it extends to virtual hugs and expressions of love and support through social media and other platforms.
