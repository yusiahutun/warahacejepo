Emily Ratajkowski Got a Head Start on Valentine's Day Dressing With an Itsy-Bitsy Heart-Print Bikini

May 07, 2024
blog

Forget chocolates and flowers, because Emily Ratajkowski is celebrating Valentine's Day a little differently this year. And while she may be single, that isn't stopping the model from partaking in the season of love with an epic look that's a clear nod to the romantic holiday. 

On Friday, EmRata revealed that she left the chilly New York weather behind and jetted off to an undisclosed tropical location with a set of photos on Instagram, captioned: "tundra escapes." In the snaps, Emily is seen soaking up the sun while wearing an itsy-bitsy bikini that included a blue string top with a red-heart print and a matching pair of high-cut bottoms. She accessorized the tiny two-piece with a gold chain and a round onyx pendant hanging from a red piece of thread.

Meanwhile, her beachside beauty look consisted of barely-there makeup that showed off her natural freckles and a tousled mane with windswept curtain bangs.

EmRata's latest bikini moment comes just days after she declared she doesn't "give a fuck" what anyone thinks about her skimpy style. “In the past two years, how I feel is I just care a whole lot less what people think,” she said during a recent interview with Glamour UK. “So, if I am in a certain type of mood where I want to post something or wear something that I could be judged for because it’s, quote-unquote ‘desperate’, I’m a little bit like, ‘Well, that’s where I’m at’. I’ve learned to prioritize my own happiness."

She added, "I feel I have bigger fish to fry than caring about the politics of whether or not I’m appealing to the male gaze in a given moment."
