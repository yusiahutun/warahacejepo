Live Nation Expands Concert Week 2024 To More Than 20 Nations Around The World

June 28, 2024
blog

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — For the tenth year in a row, Live Nation announced the return of Concert Week, a promotion event where fans can purchase tickets to more than 5,000 shows for just $25 dollars.

To mark the 10th anniversary of the program, Live Nation expanded the promotional event to more than 20 countries, including China, Singapore, Taiwan, Thailand, Austria, Belgium, Czech Republic, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Hungary, Italy, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, United Arab Emirates, Australia, New Zealand, United States, Canada, England and Scotland.

The particpating concerts for 2024 include performances by 21 Savage, Alanis Morissette, Cage The Elephant, Celeste Barber, Dan + Shay, Dierks Bentley, Feid, Janet Jackson, Meghan Trainor, New Kids On The Block, Peso Pluma, Sean Paul, and Thirty Seconds to Mars, among others.

Fans can find participating shows from May 8-14 by filtering their search on LiveNation.com/ConcertWeek by participating events, venues, or artists. While on the website, fans can also set the location to the closest city and the site will refresh to only include participating shows nearby.

Concert week tickets are all-in at $25, meaning that all fees are included up front in the price. Taxes will be added at checkout as applicable to each state. Tickets are limited and will only be made available while inventory lasts.
