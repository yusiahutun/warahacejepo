Frequently asked questions about best gel hairstyles

  • Are there gel hairstyles for natural hair? Yes, there are so many gel hairstyles for ladies with natural hair.
  • Can you use virgi*n hair for packing gel? Yes. Packing gel hair can be applied on virgi*n hair or natural 4C African hair The key is to use a medium-to-strong grip styling gel to damp or recently washed hair and style it as usual.
  • Is hair gel safe? Yes, hair gel is safe but do not use too much and avoid everyday use.
  • How long does gel stay in your hair? How long gel stays on your hair depends on the kind of gel that you use and your sebum production. Typically, it can stay from three days to a week.
  • Do you put gel on wet or dry hair? You should put gel on wet hair to avoid hair damage and dryness.
  • Should I wash my hair after using gel? Yes, you should wash your hair every time after using gel to prevent hair damage and product buildup.
  • Does hair gel damage hair? Yes, hair gels can impact normal sebum production and dry out and dehydrate your hair. Your hair may become unruly and frizzy as a result.
