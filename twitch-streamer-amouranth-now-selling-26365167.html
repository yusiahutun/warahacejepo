Twitch streamer Amouranth is now selling her farts in a jar for 750 a pop

April 06, 2024
blog

The Internet has made it possible for people to earn a living in ways nobody ever thought possible.

Amouranth, the most popular female streamer on Twitch, is the perfect example of this.

As well as owning a petrol station, making thousands on Twitch, and even $1 million per month on OnlyFans, Amouranth is now expanding her empire into scents by selling her farts in a jar for $1000 (£750).

The 28-year-old streamer, who is well-known for her ASMR and cosplay content (as well as infamous hot tub streams), has begun selling 'limited edition scent jars'. They're signed and include one of her hairs and a bespoke fart.

The jars, which are called 'Cutie Pa-Toot-ies Scents by Amouranth', can be preordered now. If you can't afford a fart, you can pick up some of her used hot tub water for the bargain price of $199.99 (£150).

In a launch video on YouTube, Amouranth (real name Kaitlyn Siragusa) demonstrated how the jars are filled. After delivering a made-to-order waft of gas, she seals the jars 'for freshness' then signs and numbers it from 1-1000. It's unclear whether shipping is included free of charge.

"Have you ever wondered what your favourite streamer smells like? Now's your chance to find out!" Amouranth said on the Cutie Pa-Toot-ies website, where you can preorder one of her scent jars.

The website also explains the production process for the 'perfume' empire. It says: "This is the Cutie Pa-Toot-ies team’s first attempt at bottling and capturing clouds, there may be production lead time involved with making sure the bottled scents can be transported safely, which may increase production time."

It adds: "We at Cutie Patooties believe that the best farts are those that are naturally occurring. Thus, the team works tirelessly to bottle and collect the clouds whenever the right moment strikes.

"It is our highest priority to ensure Amouranth is well taken care of and given the highest quality of life in exchange for her efforts. We have ensured she is able to live free range and in the highest quality conditions with little daily life interruptions, with our team’s only intervention occurring when it is time to capture a cloud."

Some of Amouranth's fans are keen to get onboard, with one commenter saying it "smells like a good deal."

Another complained about the bottling procedure, saying "You let all the fart escape, I am not gonna buy a fart less fart jar wtf.”

Farting in a jar is not the safest line of business. Another fart bottler, Steph Matto, 31, earned around $110,000 (£147,000) from farting in jars but was forced to retire after having a 'heart attack' from squeezing out one too many.

Amouranth is one of Twitch's most highly-paid streamers, earning an average of $133,000 (£99,000) every month for her bikini-clad hot tub streams where she cooks steak and talks to viewers.

Last year, Twitch threatened to 'demonetize' her account by removing ad revenue from her page. Her main account was even banned from the platform for the fifth time.

This doesn't seem to have stopped her, as she still earns roughly $1 million (£750,000) per month from OnlyFans.
