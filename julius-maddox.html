Records

On August 31, 2019, Julius Maddox beat Kirill Sarychev’s record of 738.5 pounds (335.0 kg) by pressing 739.6 pounds (335.5 kg) while wearing wrist wraps and without the use of a belt. Eric Spoto’s previous record, which was set in 2013, was 722 pounds (327 kg). With 10 repetitions, Maddox also holds the world record for the most times in a recognized powerlifting competition in that a bench press of 700 pounds (320 kg) is performed.

Maddox has recently competed at a body weight of between 440 and 460 pounds (200 to 210 kilograms). At a Ghost Strong competition in Miami, Florida, on February 21, 2021, Julius set a new All-Time World Record by lifting 782 lbs (355 kg) in weight.
