Mel B has more to say about her '90s hookup with fellow Spice Girl Geri Halliwell.

After decades of speculation, the "America's Got Talent" judge a.k.a Scary Spice finally came clean and confessed she had a fling with Halliwell (formerly known as Ginger Spice), during the group's heyday. The confession intially came during a sit-down interview Sunday with Piers Morgan.

Mel went on to clarify her comments while appearing on 2Day FM radio show Monday and reiterated that her little tryst with Halliwell wasn't a "big deal."

"It was just one of those things that happened one night and I didn't go into detail about it at all," Mel said. "I just said it was a little thing and we giggled about it the next day and that's that.

The singer also spoke about the conversation she had with Halliwell following her interview with Morgan. "I just texted her and I said, 'Look I admitted on Piers that we had a little bit of a thing but don't worry about it. It was all tongue-in-cheek and I didn't go into detail.' I said 'So don't worry about it'...I don't know why everyone is making such a big deal about it."

When asked if Halliwell responded back to her, Mel said, "Yeah. We had a long conversation. She texted me the night of and then we just spoke at lunch today amongst other things because we're getting ready for tour right now."

During her confession Sunday night, after admitting to the rumors, Mel laughed and said, "She's going to kill me and so's her husband."

But, Mel insisted, it was just a one-time encounter. "She's going to hate me for this because she's all posh in her country house and her husband. But it's a fact. It just happened and we just giggled at it and that was it." She also noted with a smile, "She had great boobs."

"And hopefully when Geri gets asked that, which hopefully she will after this, she won't deny it," Mel said. "Because it was just a fun thing."

When Morgan tried to dig in and ask how many times they'd been together, Mel shut him down hard. "Stop it, you pervert!" she said. "We were best friends. It just happened. Have you ever done that?"
