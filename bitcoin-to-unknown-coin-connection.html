Unveiling the Unknown Coins 

Defining Unknown Coins 

Unknown coins encompass a diverse array of cryptocurrencies beyond the household names like Bitcoin and Ethereum. Prominent Examples 

  • Altcoins: Altcoins are alternative cryptocurrencies to Bitcoin, offering various features like faster transaction speeds or enhanced privacy. Examples include Litecoin, Ripple (XRP), and Cardano (ADA).

  • Tokens and ICOs: Tokens represent assets built on existing blockchain platforms, like Ethereum’s ERC-20 tokens. ICOs, or initial coin offerings, are fundraising mechanisms that birthed many tokens, some of which have gained prominence.
