Matthew Rhys admits being 'protective' of Keri Russell during nude scenes on The Americans

June 30, 2024
blog

Life imitated art as they became a real life couple while working together on The Americans.

And though he is very familiar with the cast and crew, Matthew Rhys admitted to getting a little protective of Keri Russell while filming her more revealing scenes.

The 41-year-old British actor and 40-year-old actress sat down for a joint interview with The Hollywood Reporter published on Thursday as they revealed what it is like to have a romance both on and off-screen.

Dynamic duo: Matthew Rhys and Keri Russell  - pictured together in New York back in May 2015 - sat down for a joint interview with The Hollywood Reporter published on Thursday

As Keri films some rather racy scenes for the FX drama in front of dozens of cast and crew, her boyfriend discussed how he sometimes reacts to her stripping down.

Matthew explained: 'I get a little protective. I'm like, "Can someone get her a f***ing robe, please?! She's standing there naked, we've cut for five seconds, Jesus Christ." 

'And they're like, "Dude, this is the fourth season,"' he said with laughter.

'Can someone get her a f***ing robe, please?!': The 40-year-old actress has several graphic sex scenes on the show as her 41-year-old beau admitted to getting 'a little protective'

 As there have been several graphic sex scenes on the show, Keri did admit that she was a little hesitant of doing them at first.

She explained: 'No one wakes up on Tuesday morning at 6 a.m. and is [excited to be] like, "Hey! Nice to meet ya! OK, here we go, whoooo!" with 50 of our friends watching.'

The former star of Felicity said that she does love the different approach they take on the show compared to others when it comes to those intimate scenes.

Loved up: The two talented actors - pictured in New York back in May - have been dating since 2013 and welcomed a bouncing baby boy named Sam together in May

Keri said: 'In our show, I'm in a place of power: it's to get information from that person or to make that person feel a certain way. And that can be empowering.'

The two talented actors have been dating since 2013 and welcomed a bouncing baby boy named Sam together in May.

Keri already has two children, nine-year-old son River and four-year-old daughter Willa, from her relationship with ex-husband Shane Deary.

Busy couple: Keri and Matthew made an entrance at a state dinner at the White House on Tuesday

Last month, the pair were nominated for the first time in the lead actor and actress categories of the Emmys for the first time in the shows' four seasons. 

Keri commented: 'You get so used to being the underdog for so long, and you're comfortable in that place: no pressure, no expectations.'

Then Matthew joked:  'So the celebration was short-lived. For three minutes, we were like, "This is fantastic," then we were like, "Oh no, now people will watch. We're dead. Everyone will hate us. This is terrible."'

The 68th Annual Primetime Emmy Awards will be hosted by Jimmy Kimmel and is set to air on September 18 at 8pm EST/5pm PDT on ABC.
