12 White Sneaker Outfits for Spring

September 03, 2024
blog

What’s that feeling? Oh, nothing, just us coming back to life after a long, cold winter. Yup, spring is here, baby! It’s time to shed some unwanted layers—out go the puffer coats and heavy knits, tucked away until next year.

Yet, it remains a transitional period weather-wise; you're tired of the bulky boots, but it's too soon for summer sandals. Enter the timeless white sneaker: sleek, stylish, and versatile. And before you cry “basic,”—remember, classics are classic for a reason—they work. There's a plethora of options to choose from, ranging from chunky designs to sporty styles and everything in between. We're here to guide you in finding the perfect white sneakers and how to style them to perfection this spring.

Below, discover 12 outfits showcasing the endless possibilities this footwear offers as we step into the warmer days ahead.

Spring Suiting

A chic linen suit is a smart investment due to its versatility and numerous styling possibilities. You can wear the linen ensemble together—blazer, trousers, and vest—or mix and match each piece separately for endless outfit combinations. In this example, pairing the full three-piece suit with a “hyped” sneaker, like this pair from New Balance, lends a more relaxed feel to the otherwise buttoned-up look.

Lady Like

We love the juxtaposition in this outfit: a tailored tweed jacket paired with a sweet lace skirt epitomizes ladylike chic. Adding a pair of athletic-looking sneakers transforms the ultra-sweet look into something more laid-back. This unexpected combination strikes a perfect balance. Notably, the sneakers themselves embody this balance; they are part of a collaboration between New Balance and Miu Miu.

Nautical

This ensemble epitomizes spring staples with a chic twist. The classic Breton stripe top paired with a dark wash denim midi skirt is ultra chic. While the white sneakers add a touch of that effortless French-girl flair. Together, these elements create a look that is both fashionable and timeless.

Cool Classics

If it ain’t broke, don’t fix it—and this outfit formula always delivers. Classic light-wash blue jeans paired with a white tank top, a leather jacket, and sleek white sneakers create the perfect base. This look can be easily personalized with accessories like belts, jewelry, sunglasses, and handbags, allowing for endless customization to suit any style.

Chic Capris

Love them or hate them, capri pants are making a comeback. Em Rata champions this controversial trend with a vintage Dior set. She styles the outfit with Loewe’s “Ballet Runner” sneakers, which combine the construction of a ballet pump with the style of a 1970s track shoe. This vintage-inspired sneaker perfectly complements the retro feel of the capris with its feminine charm.

White Out

A classic white shirt dress is always ideal for transitioning from spring to summer. Perfect for those sweltering days when you want to stay cool yet look effortlessly chic. In keeping with the theme of comfort, opting for a “cool girl” shoe like the Nike Air Force One adds an edgy twist to the outfit, marrying fashion and function. 

Sporty Spice

This outfit perfectly balances sweetness with a casual edge. The denim dress, featuring puff sleeves and eyelet lace details, paired with a wicker bag, screams spring. However, the addition of chunky sneakers and tube socks introduces an athletic twist, creating a striking contrast with the dress's ultra-girly details. This combination effectively dresses down the look while keeping it fresh and on-trend.

Expect the Unexpected

This cable-knit twinset paired with a matching skirt embodies timeless prep. While one might expect the outfit to be complemented by classic white tennis shoes, such as Supergas or Keds, we appreciate the unconventional choice. Charlotte Groeneveld opted for Nike Air More Uptempo basketball sneakers instead. This footwear choice transforms the traditional “lady who lunches” look into one with a cool, street-style edge.

Power Prints

Spring is chock-full of flowy florals, typically seen in dresses and skirts. If flowers aren't quite your style, consider opting for a pair of printed pants in a light fabric like silk or cotton. Cheetah print offers a bold yet less sweet alternative to traditional floral patterns. If you find animal prints a bit daunting, pairing them with white sneakers can be a game-changer. The simplicity and casual vibe of the sneakers help tone down the boldness, making cheetah print pants much more approachable and easy to wear.

Flower Power

But hey, if you love florals, lean into it! Dress down an ultra-feminine floral midi dress with a pair of it-girl-approved sneakers. In this case, Adidas Sambas are the perfect choice. These shoes have received endorsements from fashion icons like Hailey Bieber, Kendall Jenner, and Bella Hadid, making them a trendy complement to any floral moment.

Slip Into Spring

A slip dress paired with white sneakers is a foolproof combination for spring—easy, comfortable, and cool. To give the look a relaxed edge, balance the sweetness of the dress with an oversized hoodie. This adds an extra layer of comfort while also complementing the sportiness of the sneakers.

In the Trenches

It doesn’t get more quintessentially spring than a great trench coat. Here, we see a sporty sneaker paired with a chic ensemble. Typically, the classic trench and cropped trousers might call for a ballet flat or a loafer, but adding a chunky sneaker brings a casual and effortless vibe to the look. This twist makes the outfit feel more relaxed and easy.
