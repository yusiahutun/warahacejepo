Gwyneth Paltrow Pretty Much Wore a Wedding Dress Covered in Diamonds

June 10, 2024
blog

Gwyneth Paltrow may have gotten all dressed in white in honor of her latest outing — but she wasn’t headed to the chapel.

On Tuesday, the actress actually opted to sport what very well could have doubled as a chic wedding dress when attending the opening of Swarovski's new Fifth Avenue flagship store in New York City. Dressing to the event’s glitzy and glamourous theme — the evening also served as a launch party for the new Swarovski x SKIMS collaboration — Paltrow slipped into a strapless, bustier-style white dress complete with a body-hugging fit-and-flare silhouette, which she paired with coordinating cream Ferragamo heels and cross-body silver chains. 

Taking a page out of Andie Anderson’s book, Gwyneth opted to frost herself with three layers of jaw-dropping Swarvoski necklaces. She complemented her look by styling her signature blonde tresses down straight with a middle part. A dewy, goop-approved complexion, feathered lashes, and a light pink lip rounded out the A-lister’s glam.

Gwyneth’s outing comes amid a busy (and stylish!) week for the star, as she also attended the CFDA Fashion Awards in NYC just a day prior. Not only did the goop founder look stunning during the ceremony — she wore a black turtleneck gown from her brand’s G. Label paired with glitzy drop earrings — but she also revealed the one actor who could convince her to return to acting amid her current years-long hiatus.

“Well, it would be very hard for me to do any acting right at the moment, just because of my job, but I guess Robert Downey Jr. could probably always get me back,” she told Entertainment Tonight of her former Iron Man co-star before clarifying, “You know, to some degree.”
