Meet Aubree Bouché the lead actress in Lifetime’s new film A Dangerous Affair.

A Dangerous Affair, the latest romantic-thriller offering from Lifetime, is written and directed by Canadian actress and writer-director Christie Will Wolf.

The network teased the drama to come: “Pilates instructor Amelie Didot begins a steamy love affair with one of her handsome clients, a Brit journalist, and media mogul Pierce Dalton, but the duo soon find themselves dangerously intertwined after Am lie comes face to face with a masked woman following a murder. When it’s revealed the killer has an erotic obsession with Pierce, Amelie becomes a target in the middle of a deadly love triangle.”

Learn about her actress’s age, husband & marriage, height, and net worth in this article.

Aubree Bouché On A Dangerous Affair

Actress Aubree Bouché plays Amélie Didot, a pilates instructor, who relocates to a new city in the course of the film in an effort to put her traumatic divorce behind her and begin over. She engages in a passionate affair with one of her clients, successful journalist Pierce Dalton, just as her Pilates class is starting to gain popularity.

When Pierce’s ex-girlfriend enters the picture, their romance takes a perilous turn. Her unrequited love for Pierce stokes resentment and jealousy, which makes her quest to win him back lethal.

Accompanying Aubree in this movie is Charlie Bewley as Pierce Dalton, Karlee Eldridge as Fran Gibbons, Tanya Christiansen as Barbara Affair, and Jevon White as Kip Green along with others. The show was filmed in Miami, according to director Christie which she revealed on her Instagram after wrapping the filming.

“I was blessed with an absolutely fantastic crew … our committed and beyond creative/talent director of photography … the most creative and hands-on producers … and, of course, beyond-talented leads … paired with an incredible supporting cast,” Christie captioned the post.

Around the same time, Aubree posted an Instagram homage to her coworkers on the movie, which was then known as Her Obsession. “To the cast and crew, thank you for making a dream come true for me, and I can’t wait to work with each of you again soon,” she wrote. “We made some Miami magic.”

The film premiered on 6 August 2022.

Is Aubree Bouché Married?

Yes, Aubree Bouché is a married woman. She exchanged vows with her husband Julio Aguiar on 7 March 2021. She announced her wedding a month later with an IG post. The married couple had a tiny ceremony and a simple wedding.

She wrote to her husband on the post, “There are always butterflies in my stomach with you. There aren’t enough words to describe how much I love you, but luckily we have forever together for me to continue to tell you and show you how much I truly do. Eu ti amo”.

Julio proposed to Aubree on 16 November 2020. The actress announced her engagement via a post. she announced, “We’re ENGAGED! I have zero doubt I want to marry you. Zero doubt you are supposed to be my partner in life. We have each gone through some wildlife events and changes and stuck by each other, lifting one another up. I am my best version of myself with you. My heart is so full of pure joy & excitement for how our lives will continue to shift and grow.”

The married couple started dating in 2017. Less than two years into dating, they signed the lease for their first place together in January 2019. They moved into the place by the end of 2019.

In February 2021, the couple adopted a new addition to their family, their dog Jojo.

In July 2020, on Julio’s 30th birthday, Aubree wished, “He’s officially 30, flirty, and thriving! Happy birthday to my best friend and partner in life. I love you with all my heart”. By profession, Julio is an artist and you can discover his artworks here (@juliu_sart).
