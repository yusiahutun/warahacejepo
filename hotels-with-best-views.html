The world's best hotel views hotels with a view

August 06, 2024
blog

If you like your vistas green and rolling, check in to Castelfalfi near Pisa and Florence in Tuscany, and take in the verdant countryside from your room in the newly renovated main house. The scenic surroundings (all 2,700 acres of them) hardly look real – you could be staring at a landscape painting instead.

The hotel is set within a historic mediaeval borgo, complete with a castle, a tabaccaia (tobacco warehouse), olive groves and vineyards. More modern additions include a spa residence by Thai wellness clinic Rakxa, a golf course and designer boutiques in the village, which include a Whyci Milano store (otherwise only found in Milan and Portofino).

The new suites eschew more traditional Tuscan decor in favour of a high-gloss, international feel, which is rare in this rural part of Italy.
