FAQs

1. What are the opening hours of Odense Zoo?

The opening hours of Odense Zoo vary depending on the season. It is recommended to check their official website for the most up-to-date information on opening hours.

2. Are there any special events or shows at Odense Zoo?

Yes, Odense Zoo regularly organizes special events and shows. These can include animal feeding sessions, educational talks, and even nighttime experiences. Again, it is advisable to check their website for upcoming events.

3. Can I bring my own food to Odense Zoo?

Yes, visitors are allowed to bring their own food and have a picnic in designated areas within the zoo. However, there are also several restaurants and food stalls available on-site where you can purchase snacks and meals.

4. Are there any services for visitors with disabilities?

Yes, Odense Zoo is committed to providing accessibility for all visitors. They have wheelchair access to all major areas, disabled toilets, and designated parking spaces. Additionally, a limited number of wheelchairs are available for rent at the entrance.

5. Can I purchase tickets online?

Yes, Odense Zoo offers the option to purchase tickets online. This allows visitors to skip the queues and go straight into the zoo. Online ticket purchases also frequently offer discounted rates.

6. Is photography allowed inside the zoo?

Yes, photography is allowed for personal use. However, commercial photography or filming requires prior permission from the zoo’s management.
