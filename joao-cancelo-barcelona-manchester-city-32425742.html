Barcelona eye up another Man City star and reckon they can get him for 20million

July 09, 2024
blog

Barcelona reckon they can pick up Manchester City's full-back Joao Cancelo for as little as £20million in the summer.

Cash-strapped Barca have been pinching pennies in recent windows, and that trend looks set to continue this year. Cancelo, 29, is currently on loan in Catalonia, but they're hoping to make that stay permanent.

According to the Sunday Mirror, Barca are confident they can land Cancelo, who was recently cleared to return to proffesional football after a heart scare, for as little as £20m. The report also suggests that Cancelo is keen to extend his stay at Barcelona.

Cancelo had a public falling out with Pep Guardiola last winter, and Guardiola shipped him out on loan to Bayern Munich for the remainder of City's treble winning season. However, the Bavarian giants opted not to trigger their £62m option to buy.

The full-back wasn't given a look-in when he return to Manchester, and he was again moved out on loan to Barcelona.

Barca have been cash strapped in recent seasons, but their president Joan Laporta has admitted he won't be forced into selling players: "We don’t want to sell any big player.

"If all goes to plan with our goals, we won’t be forced to sell any player...I think they will continue. I hope that Atletico de Madrid will not put any objection to Joao Felix continuing with us and I think that City will not object [to Cancelo] either," he told Mundo Deportivo.

"We have an agent there who is working on the issue, Jorge Mendes, and he knows our intention to continue with the players. What I can't tell you now, because it will depend a lot on the amounts, is whether we are going to sign them or extend the loan.

"It will surely be an extension of the transfer, already setting a price."
