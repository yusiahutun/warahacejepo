Porn stars recreate themselves as life-size sex dolls for HIRE taking fantasy to a new level

May 10, 2024

PORNSTARS are making erotic dreams a reality by recreating themselves as life-size sex dolls available for HIRE.

Instagram influencers and OnlyFans stars have signed up to have their replica made, starting from $600/£437 to rent for three days.

And the company behind it warn returns will work just like a library, with late fees if you don't send the doll back on time.

Inferno founder Ben Stroud told The Sun that 2022 will be seen as the year when adult entertainment "changed forever".

"In a few years this will be the norm with sex dolls being an accepted part of people's lives," he said.

"And what we are seeing is sexual relations with dolls is not just a replacement for real life sex but a separate standalone sexual experience in itself.

"We don't just see ourselves as a sex toy company but as helping bring about fundamental societal change which is why we decided to start renting our dolls out, giving millions of people across the world a chance to be part of this revolution."

Everything from bust size, to back and bum hair, have been copied.

Pornstar, body builder and wrestler Brandi Mae is among the list on board, saying she "could not put it down" when she saw hers.

"To be one of the first people in the world to have a doll created on my likeness feels like an honour and a gamechanger.

Others include English pornstar Sophie Anderson and her boyfriend Damian Oliver, as well as Thor Johnson, Anisyia, Ava Rose, Ciren Verde, Dee Siren, Alberto Blanco, Steven Barrett, and trans sex star Jelena Vermilion.

The dolls are available to hire for three days, five days or seven days, and are fully sanitised each time.

If you want to keep your own, it'll cost you $10,000/£7,308.

Inferno's owner says soon enough people will be able to order their favourite celebrity, newsreader and even politicians.

"The possibilities are endless and we already getting requests for all types of people, we will release the most popular requests for each country later in the year," he told The Sun.

"We have come a long way from the self-inflating blow up dolls Del Boy was trying to sell in Only Fools and Horses, which actually seared sex dolls into the country's public imagination.

"At the moment a lot of the people who have agreed to have a doll of theirs on the market are part of the adult entertainment industry because they are a more open market but it wont be long before mainstream celebrities get in on the act when they realise sex dolls are here to stay.

"Newsreaders, politicians and anyone with a public profile will have their own doll for sale in a few years time."

The company's next area of focus is striking franchise deals across the globe, seeking partners with local knowledge of what gets people hot under the collar in other countries.
