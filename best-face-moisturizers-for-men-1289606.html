    Why Should Men Use a Face Moisturizer?

    The importance of incorporating CTMS (cleanser, toner, moisturizer, sunscreen) into the daily skincare routine is huge, with great emphasis on AM and PM hydration. Besides, skincare today isn’t restricted to women, and men have started pampering their skin like they should. Hence, the reasons why men should moisturize daily are plenty. Below are some benefits of using a face moisturizer for men:

    1. For Dryness Relief: First and foremost, moisturizing the skin soothes dryness. It’s important to keep our skin hydrated to keep the skin barrier protected and prevent environmental aggressors from harming it 

    2. For Antioxidant And Sun Protection: Some moisturizers like the ones listed above contain added ingredients like antioxidants, plant extracts, oils, etc. that help protect the skin from environmental damage. Plus, a few moisturizers also come with an SPF formula that helps your skin stay healthy and soft even when you are out under the sun. 

    3. To Maintain Skin Balance: Daily use of lightweight and oil-free moisturizers can help combat pimples. Contrary to popular belief, moisturizers don’t cause breakouts (though, you must buy one that’s non-comedogenic and oil-free). Non-greasy and non-comedogenic moisturizers help control sebum production, thereby preventing the occurrence of acne breakouts. 

    4. To Make Skin Younger: Moisturizers seal in the nourishment from skincare products. Using the best face moisturizer for men can help them absorb active ingredients from other products in a much better way, revealing younger-looking and smoother skin. 

    What to Look for When Buying the Best Face Moisturizer for Men?

    Getting your hands on the best men’s face moisturizer can seem overwhelming at first, but it won’t once you know the necessary points to keep in mind before buying one. Below is a guide to help you make an informed decision:

    1. Know Your Skin Type: 

    It is very important to invest in a moisturizer that suits your skin type. If you have dry skin, go for moisturizers packed with emollients and humectants like shea butter, sunflower oil, jojoba oil, hyaluronic acid, aloe vera, panthenol, cica, vitamin E, caprylic triglyceride, urea, glycerin, etc. Those with oily skin should stick to lightweight formulas that won’t leave any unwanted shine behind. Hypoallergenic products are the best pick for those with skin sensitivities. 

    2. Identify Your Skin Concern:

    As mentioned above, moisturizers can have the power to do more than just hydrate. If acne is your concern, pick a lightweight and non-greasy moisturizer packed with anti-acne ingredients. For those dealing with constant acne breakouts, we would suggest using the Cardon Daily SPF + Moisturizer. Likewise, those dealing with fine lines and other signs of aging should look for a moisturizer with anti-aging benefits. The Brickell Men's Products Daily Essential Face Moisturizer and the Clinique For Men Anti-age Moisturizer have the best age-defying ingredients. In a nutshell, go through the label to understand if the particular product would address your skin concern or not and make the purchase accordingly. 

    3. Consider a Product with SPF:

    Buying a moisturizer with a SPF formula has a lot of benefits. If you spend a lot of time outdoors, it’s advisable to invest in a moisturizer that will defend your skin against UV rays. For instance, getting your hands on the Cardon Daily SPF + Moisturizer will make sure that you don’t need any sunscreen. It will not only moisturize your skin but also keep the signs of aging at bay by protecting your skin from sun damage.

    FAQ's

    Can men with oily and acne-prone skin use a face moisturizer?

    Yes, of course, they can. They just need to ensure that they use a lightweight, non-comedogenic, and oil-free formula.

    Can men use face moisturizers after shaving?

    Yes, there are a few moisturizers that are specifically made to be used post-shaving and those can be used by men.

    Can men use the same face moisturizer as women?

    The skin of a man and a woman is different, hence, it’s best to use moisturizers that are for men or unisex.

    Are there any tips to keep in mind before using face moisturizer?

    Make sure to use a face moisturizer that suits your skin type and if you are going out in the sun, it’s best to use a moisturizer with SPF.
