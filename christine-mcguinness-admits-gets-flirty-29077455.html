Christine McGuinness has admitted she is "open" to dating - but is in no "big rush" to jump into a new relationship following her split from hubby Paddy last year.

The TV personality, 34, said she has has received flirty messages from unnamed celebrities on her DMs, but that she has not been interested in pursuing matters further.

Christine and Paddy announced last year they had parted company after 11 years of marriage, and said of the mystery stars who had contacted her: "They kinda drop in like: 'Hey, do you wanna go out?'"
