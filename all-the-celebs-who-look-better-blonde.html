Margot Robbie

Are you just as shocked as us finding out Margot Robbie is actually a natural brunette? The Australian actress is known for her signature blonde tresses in addition to being Hollywood’s favorite bombshell. I mean, think about her major-motion-picture moments like playing Noami in The Wolf of Wall Street or Harley Quinn in Suicide Squad—the people love to see her blonde! Robbie continues her craft (as a brunette this time) in historical comedy Amsterdam, released this past October.

Kim Kardashian

Stefanie Keenan//Getty Images

If looks could kill, it’s Kim Kardashian in her blonde era. As someone with naturally dark hair, she revealed on a recent episode of Live with Kelly and Ryan that it takes up to eight hours to bleach her roots. Kim also shared that she’s “going to stay blonde for a minute,” which is totally fine with us! The Skims founder has been rocking platinum blonde hair ever since her Met Gala 2022 look. Since then, she’s even bleached her eyebrows to match in a shoot with Interview Magazine and continues to embrace the Marilyn Monroe vibes at Milan Fashion Week, where she curated the Dolce & Gabbana SS23 Show.
