Jose Marie Borja Viceral (born March 31, 1976), known professionally as Vice Ganda (lit. '"Beautiful Vice"'), is a Filipino comedian, talk show host, television presenter, actor, and singer. He was a regular host-judge on ABS-CBN's noontime variety show It's Showtime, and has starred in several films, eight of which are considered to be the highest-grossing in Philippine cinema.
