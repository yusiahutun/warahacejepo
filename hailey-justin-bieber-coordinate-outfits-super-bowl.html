Hailey Bieber Channels the Mob Wife Trend for a Super Bowl Date with Justin

March 11, 2024
blog

While football fans had their eyes on the field, we had our eyes on the stands, where there were celebrities in hordes, from Taylor Swift hanging with pals Ice Spice and Brittany Mahomes to Beyoncé and Jay-Z watching the game with their kids. Of course, we immediately spotted one of the most stylish couples in attendance—Justin and Hailey Bieber—who coordinated in a pair of bold patterned outfits.

For Hailey’s part, she wore a white ringer tee underneath an oversized cheetah print coat with broad shoulders and wide lapels—the latter being the latest celebrity contribution to the viral mob wife trend. And in typical Hailey fashion, she paired the top with wide-leg jeans and added a surprise pop of color with ruby-colored kitten heels from Jil Sander. Although they were hard to spot through Hailey’s curtain bangs, the Rhode founder also accessorized with a pair of earrings from Tiffany & Co.

PATRICK T. FALLON//Getty Images

Meanwhile, her beau showed up in an equally eye-popping print. Justin looked like he was showing off his astrology side with his caramel colored button-up top, which featured a pattern of crescent moons printed across it. The “Peaches” singer paired the top with dark wash jeans which featured a thin white stripe going down the side and he accessorized with a black baseball cap, which he wore backwards. In photos from the pair’s arrival to the stadium, Justin could also be seen sporting a pair of burgundy loafers and a black leather jacket, which he took off once inside.
