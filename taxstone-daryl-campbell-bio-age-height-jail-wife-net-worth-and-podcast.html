Taxstone Jail

On 16 January 2017, Daryl was jailed in connection to the shooting death of Ronald “Edgar” McPhatter, Ronald was working at the time as the bodyguard for rapper Roland Collins (Troy Ave.) at an Irving Plaza show for fellow rapper Clifford Joseph Harris Jr. (T.I.) Daryl was indicted on charges of being a felon in possession of a firearm as well as receiving a gun through interstate commerce. Authorities allege that Daryl’s DNA was found on the trigger, handgrip, and magazine of the Nine-millimeter Kel-Tec semiautomatic handgun that was used in the shooting in the venue’s green room.

Taxstone Podcast

Daryl caught the attention of The Loud Speakers Network’s podcast hosts and on January 8, 2015, Daryl appeared on an episode of Charlamagne Tha God and Andrew Schulz’s The Brilliant Idiots podcast.

Another podcast host on the network, Kid Fury, had also recommended Daryl to the network’s bosses Combat Jack and Chris Morrow which led him to land his own podcast Tax Season.

Daryl released the first episode of Tax Season on The Loud Speakers Network on March 18, the year 2015, just four months after Daryl was first recommended to the network by Kid Fury.
