UFC ring announcer salary

How much does the ring announcer make in the UFC? The Ultimate Fighting Championship gives announcers a chance to make money on two events; regular matches and pay-per-view matches.

During a regular UFC match, a professional announcer (contract based) is paid $5,000. The figure is way lower than what they receive from pay-per-view (PPV) events. Even though the PPV events are not for anyone, the lucky to land such gigs take home between $50,000 to $100,000.

What does being a UFC ring announcer entail?

Ring announcers are paid employees for a boxing, professional wrestling, or mixed martial arts event. Their primary duties are to introduce the participants involved in the scheduled matches to the viewing audience. In addition, they often state the game's rules, and when the fight concludes, they announce the winner.
