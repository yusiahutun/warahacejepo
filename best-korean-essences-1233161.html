What is Korean Essence?

A Korean essence can be termed as a softener which comes between your toner and serum. It makes the skin more supple and opens up the pores, letting the serum and moisturizer go deep inside the skin and work its magic. Although it is water-based, neither it is as light and watery as a toner nor its consistency is as concentrated as a serum. Its main aim is to provide extreme hydration to the skin.

What Exactly Does Korean Essence Do And Why Should You Use It?

A Korean essence is mainly used for hydrating the skin. Infused with skin-caring ingredients and botanicals, it assists in magnifying the benefits of serum and moisturizer. The lightweight texture aids in skin repair which promotes smoother and brighter skin. Use it after cleansing to let maximum absorption of the highly concentrated treatment delivered by the active ingredients present in serums. Formulated with hyaluronic acid, glycerine, and other hydrating agents, it soaks your skin with extra moisture while providing your skin with a strengthened barrier, balanced pH, and increased elasticity.

    Why Use A Korean Essence At All?

    Korean essence provides an added layer of moisture, drenching the skin with hydration which is specifically needed for mature skin. It helps in bringing the correct pH that balances with the skin. It is applied before a serum or moisturizer as it makes the skin more porous to absorb their benefits. An essence also increases the skin density, strengthens the skin barrier, and improves the skin texture. Korean essence products are infused with fermented ingredients that produce lactic acid and probiotics that help in retaining skin moisture and balance the microbiomes.

    FAQ's

    Is Korean essence the same as a toner?

    No, Korean essence is not the same as a toner. A toner helps in maintaining the skin's pH balance while an essence provides the skin with hydration.

    Is facial essence necessary?

    Facial essence hydrates and soothes thirsty skin cells, enhancing the skin's capacity to absorb the skincare products for better results.

    Is Korean facial essence the best?

    Korean facial essence provides an added layer of moisture to the skin which is beneficial for dry skin. It also helps in reducing fine lines and wrinkles, and strengthens skin barrier in sensitive skin.

    Can I use Essence every day?

    As essence is water-based and its main aim is to provide hydration to the skin, it can be used 1-2 times a day, preferably during the morning and nighttime skincare routine.

    Can a Korean essence help with hydration and moisturization?

    Yes, Korean essence helps with hydration and moisturization.

    Which is better – essence or serum?

    Essence and serum, both play different parts in your skincare routine. An essence offers hydration while a serum uses active ingredients that penetrate deep into the skin to work their benefits.

    hat goes before and after an essence?

    An essence is applied after cleansing and toning the face. And after the essence sets into the skin, a serum is applied.

    Can I skip toner and use essence?

    Essence should be applied after toner. Toner is not advisable to skip as it clears away any impurities that might have stayed behind after cleansing.

    Which is the better essence or moisturizer?

    Both essence and moisturizer have their own benefits. An essence provides the skin with hydration while a moisturizer helps in locking in that moisture.

    How long does it take to see results from using a Korean essence?

    Regular and consistent use of Korean Essence shows visible results in 2 to 3 weeks.
