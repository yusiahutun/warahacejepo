Bravo's "Real Housewives" are known for their drama, and Thursday night's episode did not disappoint.

The seven New York housewives -- Ramona Singer, Jill Zarin, Alex McCord, LuAnn de Lesseps, Kelly Bensimon, Sonja Morgan and Cindy Barshop -- were vacationing in Morocco when disaster nearly struck.

During what supposed to be a leisurely caravan through the desert, de Lesseps' camel began bucking wildly, nearly throwing the former Countess off. The drama was caught on multiple cameras. Watch the video below:

The camel calmed down, but became agitated a second time. That was enough for de Lesseps, who promptly dismounted from the animal. "I'm very happy to get off" the normally reserved former Countess admitted. "I was scared!"

Jill Zarin also had problems with the four-legged rides when another camel ate her bracelet.

Speaking of animals, the claws come out during "The Real Housewives of O.C." reunion. In a preview for the explosive episode, Tamra Barney accuses fellow castmember Gretchen Rossi of sleeping with another guy (Slade Smiley) while her ex-fiance was dying from leukemia.
