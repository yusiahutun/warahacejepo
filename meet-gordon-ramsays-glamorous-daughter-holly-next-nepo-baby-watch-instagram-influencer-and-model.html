Holly Anna Ramsay comes from celebrity chef royalty

Holly was born on New Year’s Day, at the turn of the millennium in 2000, to a big family – and she even has a twin. She’s the daughter of Hell’s Kitchen celebrity chef Gordon Ramsay, and Tana Ramsay, a TV broadcaster and author of several cookbooks.

Both of her parents are British and they got married in 1996 before steadily growing their household: Megan, 25, twins Holly and Jack, 23, Matilda aka “Tilly”, 21, and Oscar, 4.

Tragically, the couple lost another child, Rocky, five months into Tana’s pregnancy.

She was studying fashion design – but left due to PTSD

According to British media, Ramsay was studying fashion design at London’s Ravensbourne University, but had to leave after her first year. Sources stated that she was admitted to Nightingale Hospital, where she spent three months as an inpatient, and was diagnosed with anxiety, PTSD and depression.

It was later revealed that she had been sexually assaulted when she was 18, per Independent. On her podcast 21 & Over, she said: “The PTSD was a result of two sexual assaults when I was 18. I didn’t tell anyone about it until a year afterwards. I just buried it in a box in the back of my mind.”

She hosts a podcast about her life and struggles with depression

Ever since this incident, Ramsay has made a point to be vocal about her experiences and provide a platform to support those in need. She launched her podcast 21 & Over, available on Apple Podcasts, Spotify and iHeartRadio, where she talks, often with guests, about dealing with the struggles of mental health.

According to Studio Ramsay, the aim of the podcast is to “create a kind, healthy and safe conversation to inspire healing and emotional well-being”. The last episode, however, was released in August 2021, so it’s unclear whether the show is still active.

She’s also opened up about her sobriety, saying on her Instagram that, “Living without alcohol has helped me feel better and more present both mentally and physically.”

She’s a model and influencer

Ramsay has become an Instagram star in her own right. With over 330,000 followers, the digital creator shares snippets of her life, from family days out and boat trips to events like the Wiba Awards in Cannes. Her TikTok also boasts a cool 300,000-plus fans.

According to British media, she signed with Established Modelling Agency in 2019, and with CAA for representation late last year, per Variety. CAA is renowned for repping some of Hollywood’s biggest talents, from Ana de Armas and Anya Taylor-Joy to Bradley Cooper and Charlize Theron. The same source stated that she’s currently working on a documentary focused on sex education.
