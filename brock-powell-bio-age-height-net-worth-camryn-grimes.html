Meet Brock Powell, Camryn Grimes

Camryn Grimes and Brock Powell got engaged on 07 January 2022 (which is also Camryn’s birthday). The couple announced their engagement on 11 January on their own Instagrams with matching posts.

They both posted a photo of the two of them kissing with their joined hands extended. The hands, which are closer to the camera are in focus while the lovebirds themselves are out of focus. An engagement ring is visible on Camryn’s finger.

They both captioned their photos with lines from the 2009 Pixar animated movie called “Up”. Camryn quoted Ellie from the movie, “You and me, we’re in a club now.” And Brock quoted a character named Dug, “I have just met you & I love you…”

There was a second photo that contained a full homage to the movie. There were props inspired by the movie, ballons, and one of the most famous lines from the movie, “You will always be my greatest adventure”. And of course, there was the hashtag, “#onlyupfromhere”.

Brock and Camryn first met in 2020. We do not know the details of their early relationship, but it seems they celebrated the first anniversary of their relationship on 14 March 2021.

On social media, the couple looks picture-perfect. Apart from the Up references in their engagement, Brock and Camryn also post about them visiting places. For instance, in July 2021, they went to visit Disneyland.

If you do not know who Camryn Grimes is, she is an American actress from Los Angeles who is famous for her role of Cassie Newman and Mariah Copeland in the CBS soap opera The Young and the Restless.

In 2000, at the age of 10, she won the Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Younger Actress in a Drama Series. She has been a part of The Young and the Restless since 1997.

Apart from The Young and the Restless, Camryn has had minor roles in movies like Magic Mike and Swordfish, as well as TV shows like ER, NCIS: Los Angeles, The Mentalist, and so on.

Camryn is the daughter of Preston Lee and the niece of Scott Grimes. She has three siblings Dakota, Shelby, and Tatum.

Brock Powell Age

Brock Powell Net Worth

As of 2022, Brock Powell has a net worth of over $600 thousand.

Like, Camryn, Brock Powell is also on showbusiness. He is a voice actor and a writer. As a voice actor, he is known for giving the voice to the characters like Vela Tsubaki in Seinto Seiya: The Lost Canvas – Meio Shinwa (2011), Wilhelm Van Astrea in Re: Zero, Starting Life in Another World, and so on.

He has 64 acting credits (both voice and live-action acting) on IMDb. Brock also has two writing credits. He has written an episode each for TV shows Global Gains and The UnPOP Podshow.

Furthermore, Brock has also worked in a “progressive commercial” and voiced Kool-Aid Man.

Brock also has a little theatre experience. He has worked in plays like Into the Woods, The Tempest, A Christmas Carol, The Cherry Orchard, Anne of Green Gables, among others.

For his education, Brock went to Concordia University Irvine and graduated with a theatre degree. He was also a part of the Las Vegas branch of the Second City improv group.
