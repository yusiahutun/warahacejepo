11 of the Best Products to Combat Your Smelly Feet

August 21, 2024
blog

We’ve all experienced those those extra-hot days where we’ve taken our shoes off only to gasp at the odor. For many people, smelly feet — medically known as bromodosis — is a more regular inconvenience. In fact, a 2012 study of men and women aged 21 and up by the Institute for Preventative Foot Health showed that 16 percent (roughly 36 million people), suffer from the malodorous condition.

But stinky tootsies shouldn’t get to a point where it causes you embarrassment or keeps you from enjoying your life! There are dozens of products on the market that can curb foot and shoe odor so you can get back to more important things.

What causes feet to stink?

Everyone sweats — some more than others — but it only starts to smell when the moisture becomes trapped in your socks and shoes, leading to an overgrowth growth of bacteria or fungus. These bacteria eat your dead skin cells and digest your sweat before releasing a foul-smelling gas —the culprit behind your foot odor.

How to Get Rid of Smelly Feet

Now that you know what’s behind that foul smell, it’s time to get rid of it. There are several measures you can take to minimize the stench, beginning with prevention. Wearing moisture-wicking fabrics that allow your sweat to evaporate is a great first step you can take toward thwarting foot funk. Another is to opt for breathable shoes, which perform the same function.

You can also wash your feet with a good antibacterial soap ($14.95, Amazon) can help you put your best sweat-free foot forward.
