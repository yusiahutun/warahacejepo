Can you name these 90s footballers? Only true fans ace our picture quiz

September 15, 2024

Football in England changed considerably in the 90s.

Astronomical wages led to an influx of some of the finest foreign talent on the planet.

To add to that continental flair, there were outlandish kits and outrageous hairstyles.

But, in a perfect moment never to be repeated, the Premier League (formerly the Premiership) still boasted some of the big-boozing, tough-tackling, 'tache-sporting heroes we'd loved watching for years.

How many of the old and new guard of the 90s do you remember though?

Here, we've created a 90s football quiz to put your knowledge - and memory - to the ultimate test as you strive to pass the time during coronavirus lockdown.

Some you'll recognise instantly, others' names will be on the tip of your tongue, but a handful might have you totally stumped.

It's simple - give these 22 pictures a look and tell us who the player in each one is. Good luck!
