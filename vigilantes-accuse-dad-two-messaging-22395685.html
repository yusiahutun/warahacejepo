Vigilantes accuse dad-of-two of messaging girl, 14, as his wife asks for truth

March 24, 2024
blog

Vigilante paedophile hunters accused a dad-of-two of messaging a 14-year-old girl as his shocked wife was left asking him if it is true.

Self-proclaimed "hunting" group Broken Dreams, who describe themselves as volunteers “seeking online predators”, confronted the man outside his home in Buckinghamshire.

Thames Valley Police confirmed the arrest of a man for causing or inciting a girl, aged 13 to 15, to engage in sexual activity, according to MailOnline.

In footage live-streamed on Facebook, vigilante hunters surround the man and accuse him of messaging a kid.

The suspect, who was wearing blue shirts and a white T-shirt, is then quizzed by his wife who can be heard in the background of the clip.

Talking to the hunters, the dad-of-two insists he has “no recollection” of messaging an underage girl.

He tells them that he is taking antidepressants, and, and is a heavy drinker, and says: “'When I have had a drink and have taken anti-depressants I have no recollection of what I've done.

“I don’t remember any of that stuff. I have no recollection."

And later he says: “I don’t even know what you are talking about.”

Whilst the group confront the suspect his wife can be heard shouting “who are they”.

Later in the 37-minute video, she approaches her husband and the vigilantes and asks: “Why are you doing this to our family? What's going on here? Are you ok? Is this not true?”

The suspect continues to insist that he doesn’t recall the incidents that are being described to him, before sitting on the ground.

Hunters quiz the dad-of-two for over 25 minutes, during which his wife repeatedly asks him to come inside and explain what is going on.

Later police arrive at the scene and the man walks with them and gets into the back of one of their car.

A spokesman for Thames Valley Police told MailOnline: ”Following a call from a member of the public on Sunday, July 12, Thames Valley Police officers arrested a 43-year-old man on suspicion of causing/inciting a girl 13 to 15 to engage in sexual activity.

“He has been released on bail pending further inquiries.”
