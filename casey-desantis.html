Casey DeSantis is a beautiful, talented a skilled former American news and television host who is known as the first lady of Florida. She is preceded by Ann Scott and Governor Ron DeSantis. She assumed the office on January 8, 2019.

Early Life

Jill Casey DeSantis popularly known as Casey DeSantis was born on June 26, 1980, in Troy, Ohio, in the United States of America. Casey has been quite secretive about her family. She has never mentioned the name of her parents or she has never mentioned the name of her siblings. Casey graduated from the College of Charleston and was a 3- time National Champion equestrian as well as runner-up Division I NCAA Champion.  She received a Bachelor of Science degree in economics and minored in the French language. In college, she was a member of Omicron Delta Epsilon honor society for economics as well as Pi Delta Phi honor society in French.

Casey DeSantis Career

Casey started her career by hosting Golf Channel programs On the Tee and PGA Tour Today. She was also a local newscaster and an anchor for WJXT and held several positions including general reporter morning anchor and police reporter. Casey also did special reports including for CNN and worked as a creator- moderator for the Chat in 2014 which was cast on NBC and ABC. She even hosted the daily NBC talk show First Coast Living as well as the weekly The American Dream program about entrepreneurs. In 2019, she was given the title of First Lady of Florida. Casey presided over the first meeting of the Florida Children and Youth Cabinet as its chair.
