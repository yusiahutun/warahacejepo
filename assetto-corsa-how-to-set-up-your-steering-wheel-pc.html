How to Set Up Your Steering Wheel for Assetto Corsa on PC

September 01, 2024
blog

Assetto Corsa is a popular sim-racing game for PC that offers a realistic driving experience. Being a simulator, the best way to enjoy the Assetto Corsa experience is by using a steering wheel.

Let's learn how to set up your steering wheel for Assetto Corsa on PC.

Step 1: Connect the Steering Wheel to Your PC

The first step is to connect your steering wheel to your PC. This can be done through a USB cable or via a wireless connection, depending on the steering wheel you have. Make sure that your PC recognizes the steering wheel by checking the device manager.

For this guide, we'll be using a Logitech G29 Force Feedback Steering Wheel for demonstration. G29 connects to the PC with a USB and requires an AC adapter to power it. Make sure everything's in place and then proceed to the next step.

Step 2: Download the Necessary Software

Now that the wheel is plugged in, download some important software to help you set it up for Assetto Corsa. You'll be needing two software namely, Wheel Checker and LUT generator.

These two files can be found as executables on the official RaceDepartment website. Once you have downloaded both the software, move on to the next step i.e, generating a LUT file.

While the RaceDepartment website and its mods are trustable, as a rule of thumb, you should still thoroughly check a few things before you download mods of any kind to make sure it isn't malware .

Step 3: Generate the LUT File

This is the most important step as generating a LUT file will help in calibrating the force feedback produced by your wheel and make the overall racing experience much smoother and force-feedback response more accurate. This is one of the many things that can make simulation games more fun to play. Here are the steps to generating the LUT file:

  • Start by making sure the wheel is connected and centered.
  • Open the Wheel Checker Application. Click on Spring Force and select Step Log 2 from the drop-down menu.
  • The wheel should start turning abruptly. Just leave it be as it's part of the process.
  • After the wheel movement stops, typically, in a few minutes, open up the LUT generator application.
  • Click on Open CSV & Save LUT. A file-selection window should open up. Navigate to and select the .csv file that you just generated with the Wheel Checker application.

    • That's all the steps required to generate the LUT file for your wheel. Now let's set it up for use in the game.

    Step 4: Set Up the Wheel in Assetto Content Manager

    Fire up Assetto Content Manager(CM). It's assumed that you already have it installed as it's a must-have application without which you will not be able to reap the full potential of Assetto Corsa. In case you don't have it installed, download the Assetto CM from the official CM website. Once installed, follow these steps to set up your wheel in Assetto Corsa CM:

  • Open the Content Manager application.
  • Click on Settings > Asseto Corsa > Controls.
  • On the Controls page, select Wheel as the controller.
  • In the FFB settings, click on Use LUT file and select the LUT file which you generated in the previous step.

    • That's all the steps required to set up your force-feedback steering wheel for Assetto Corsa on PC.

    Now You Can Cruise in Assetto Corsa With Your Steering Wheel

    Now that you have successfully set up your wheel for Assetto Corsa, you will be able to enjoy an immersive driving experience. Note that using the wheel to requires a lot of concentration, and you may end up with strains due to long racing sessions.

    In case you feel too tired to use the wheel, or sometimes, may just want to chill on your couch and race casually, you can switch to using a controller. But, in time, you'll be steering like a pro.
