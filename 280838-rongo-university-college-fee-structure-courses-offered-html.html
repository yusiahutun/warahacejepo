Rongo university college fee structure &amp; courses offered

September 26, 2024
blog

Rongo University, formerly a constituent college of Moi University, is one of the fast growing institutions in the region. The university holds the record for its immense contributions in various research programs and higher learning education programs in Kenya. The number of students enrolling at Rongo University has continually surged opening new opportunities for the institution to excel. It is of interest to many people to know the Rongo University College fee structure and study programs for the year 2018/2019. Furthermore, we have availed sufficient information about the university to help you make the right decision in the limelight of your career ambitions.

The courses offered at Rongo University College have considerably increased following the charter awarded in 2016 by his excellence President Uhuru Kenyatta. The main university campus sits on Kitere hill, Migori County, which about eleven kilometers from Rongo town, and 2.5 kilometers off the Rongo-Migori highway. Rongo University College also has a satellite learning center in Msomi complex, Migori town. The university is now exercising its mandate in offering market-oriented training programs by promoting creativity and innovation as part of the curriculum. Rongo University College is keen on academic quality offering an array of courses with affordable tuition fees.
