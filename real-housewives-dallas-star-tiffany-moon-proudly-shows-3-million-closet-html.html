Real Housewives of Dallas star Tiffany Moon proudly shows off her $3 MILLION closet

July 09, 2024

Real Housewives of Dallas star Tiffany Moon has given a tour of her lavish $3 million dollar closet that is filled with designer clothes and accessories, including dozens of Hermès and Chanel handbags. 

The 37-year-old anesthesiologist considers her custom closet to be her 'favorite room' in her 12,000 square-foot Texas mansion, and she joked that she would like to be buried inside it when she dies. 

The contents of Moon's closet are so expensive that the room has to be protected 24/7. Her husband, Daniel Moon, doesn't even have access to it. 

'We have a fingerprint lock here,' she explained on Truly's series Bling Life. 'There're only two fingerprints that can get you into this closet — one is mine.'
