Travis Kelce recently landed in Australia to support Taylor Swift on her Eras Tour, but it’s not just her he’s supporting it’s the whole crew, as one fan captured an adorable clip. We love a supportive King!

The celebrity couple first went public in September, when Taylor Swift was seen supporting Travis Kelce at a Kansas City Chiefs game. Recently, the NFL star flew over 8000 miles to support his girlfriend, and her crew, at her Eras Tour.

Travis Kelce adorably seen ‘congratulating’ Taylor’s whole team after the show

As the lights went up at Sydney’s Accor Stadium, fans were told to leave the venue as soon as possible due to weather conditions. However, that wasn’t stopping Swifties as they spotted Trav backstage.

The NFL star is back partying in Vegas
