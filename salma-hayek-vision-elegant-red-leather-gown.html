See Salma Hayek Look Gorgeous in an Elegant Red Leather Gown

February 04, 2024
blog

Salma Hayek’s latest look shows an edgier side of her timeless red-carpet style.

Yesterday, the Black Mirror star shared a gorgeous look from her recent trip to Paris, where she accompanied husband François-Henri Pinault to a dinner celebrating the 10th anniversary of French fashion conglomerate Kering, which Pinault leads as chairman and CEO. For the occasion, Hayek wore a curve-hugging red leather dress by Alexander McQueen. The midi-length piece featured an asymmetrical, off-the-shoulder neckline and a bodycon silhouette that flowed into a column skirt.

The actor added a pair of strappy silver sandals, and her accessories included a large diamond ring and sparkling drop earrings, also by Alexander McQueen. She wore her hair in a sophisticated top bun and completed the look with a ’90s-style dark nude lip.

The actor has spent this past week making several splashy red-carpet appearances. On Tuesday night, she attended the Kering Foundation’s second annual Caring for Women dinner in NYC, where she and Pinault were the star-studded event’s co-chairs. She looked incredible in a crimson red halterneck gown completely covered in glittering sequins, which included thin spaghetti straps and a mini V-neckline. She accessorized with a long rectangular clutch made of black nylon, dazzling diamond drop earrings, and a pair of strappy black sandals.

On Wednesday, the star made her way to the Toronto International Film Festival, where she attended the premiere of El Sabor de la Navidad at the Royal Alexandra Theatre. Hayek produced the Mexican Christmas family dramedy. She opted to wear a gorgeous ivory dress featuring a form-fitting sleeveless bodice, draping fabric across the waist, and a long train attached at the back. To accessorize, she added a rectangular mint green clutch with matching icy-blue and white sequins and gold hardware.
