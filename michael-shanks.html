Professional Career

Michael Shanks started his acting career as guest appearances on shows including “Highlander” and “The Commish.” Moreover, he appeared in the television movies “A Family Divided” and “The Call of the Wild.”

Meanwhile, his role received much appreciation for his acting ability in this series from both critics as well as the public.
